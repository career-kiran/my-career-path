Chartered Accountant

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine you started a new business or shop.
  • Every month, the business earns money, spends money, and needs to pay taxes to the government.
  • Someone should carefully check all the accounts and make sure everything is correct.
  • That person is called a Chartered Accountant (CA).
  • 👉 In simple words: Chartered Accountants help businesses manage money, accounts, and taxes properly.

🎓 How to Become a Chartered Accountant

  • There are two official entry routes to join the Chartered Accountancy (CA) program.
  • Pick the one that matches your current educational qualification
STEP 1

Pass Class 12 from any stream (Commerce, Science, or Arts).

STEP 2

Register for the CA Foundation course through the ICAI website.

ICAI Official Website
STEP 3

Study for the CA Foundation exam for the required study period.

STEP 4

Pass all papers of the CA Foundation examination.

STEP 5

Register for the CA Intermediate course.

STEP 6

Study for the Intermediate level and complete ICITSS training.

STEP 7

Pass both groups of the CA Intermediate examination.

STEP 8

Start 2-year Articleship training under a practicing Chartered Accountant.

STEP 9

Register for the CA Final course and complete self-paced modules.

STEP 10

Pass the CA Final exam and become a Chartered Accountant.
