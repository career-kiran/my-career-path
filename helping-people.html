Helping People

Careers where you care for, support, guide, heal, or improve the well-being of others. These roles focus on helping people directly through healthcare, support, and service.

Doctor

Diagnose and treat illnesses.

Nurse

Care for patients and support recovery.

Dentist

Take care of teeth and oral health.

Physiotherapist

Help people recover movement and strength.

Counsellor / Psychologist

Support mental and emotional well-being.

Nutritionist / Dietitian

Improve health through food and nutrition.

Pharmacist

Provide medicines and guide safe use.

Healthcare Support Roles

Support doctors and nurses in patient care.

Public Health / Community Health Worker

Improve health at community level.

AYUSH Doctor

Dental Laboratory Technician

Lab Technician
