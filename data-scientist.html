Data scientist

📊 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine an online shopping app company wants to know which products its customers buy the most.
  • Someone studies the data in the app and finds out which products are popular.
  • Based on this information, the app can show more of these products to customers.
  • The person who studies this data is called a Data Scientist.
  • 👉 In simple words: Data Scientists study data to understand what people like and need.

Where do Data Scientists work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in Data Science and Analytics.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

📊

Data Analyst

(After Short-Term Course / Certification)

Cleans company data and creates reports, charts, and dashboards.

🐍

Junior Data Scientist

(After Diploma / BCA / B.Tech)

Uses programming and statistics to discover patterns in data.

📈

Business Intelligence Associate

(After Diploma / Graduation)

Creates insights that help companies make better business decisions.

🗂️

Data Reporting Assistant

(After Online Course / Graduation)

Organizes company data and prepares performance reports.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

🧮

Data Scientist

(After BCA / B.Tech / BSc CS + Experience)

Builds predictive models to forecast trends, sales, and customer behavior.

🤖

Machine Learning Engineer

(After BCA / B.Tech / BSc CS + Experience)

Turns data science models into automated software systems.

📉

Predictive Analytics Specialist

(After Analytics Training + Experience)

Uses historical data to predict future business outcomes.

🏢

Business Data Consultant

(After Industry Experience)

Helps organizations solve problems using data-driven insights.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🎯

Lead / Principal Data Scientist

(After MCA / M.Tech / Ph.D. + Experience)

Leads data science teams and develops long-term data strategies.

🏛️

Director of Data Science

(After Postgraduate Program + Experience)

Connects data analytics with major business and executive decisions.

👨‍💼

Chief Data Officer (CDO)

(After Senior Leadership Experience)

Oversees the organization's entire data strategy and governance.

📊

Data Analytics Companies

🛒

E-Commerce Companies

🤖

Artificial Intelligence Companies

🏦

Banking & Financial Companies

🏥

Healthcare & Research Organizations

Remote Data Science Jobs

Work with global companies to analyze data and solve business problems.
