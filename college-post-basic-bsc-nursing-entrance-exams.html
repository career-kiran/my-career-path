College Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams

What are College Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams?

College Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams are entrance examinations conducted by individual colleges and institutions for admission to Post Basic B.Sc Nursing courses.

There is no single national entrance examination for admission to Post Basic B.Sc Nursing courses in India.

Conducted by: Individual colleges and nursing institutions.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: GNM Nursing

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by college)

Duration: Varies by College

Questions: Varies by College

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

Qualification: General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) from a recognised institution.

Registration: Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife (RN/RM).

Minimum Marks: Minimum qualifying marks may vary by college.

Experience: Some colleges may require clinical work experience.

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by college)

Duration: Varies by College

Total Questions: Varies by College

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Marking Scheme: Varies by College
