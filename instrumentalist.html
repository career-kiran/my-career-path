Instrumentalist

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine a singer is performing a song on stage or in a studio.
  • Someone is playing the musical instruments like guitar, keyboard, drums, or violin on the background to create the music.
  • That person is called an Instrumentalist.
  • 👉 In simple words: Instrumentalists play musical instruments for songs, concerts, recordings, and performances.
