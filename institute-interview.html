Institute Interview

Institute Interview is an admission process in which a college or university speaks directly with a student before offering admission.

The interview helps the institution understand the student's interest, communication skills, goals, and suitability for the course.

Example:

A student applies for a postgraduate design programme.

After reviewing the application, the institute invites the student for an interview.

During the interview, teachers may ask about the student's interests, projects, career goals, and reasons for choosing the course.

The student answers the questions and discusses their experiences and achievements.

If the interview goes well, the student may be offered admission.
