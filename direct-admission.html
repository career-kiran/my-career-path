Direct Admission

Direct Admission is a process in which students can join a course without appearing for a national or state-level entrance examination.

Students are admitted if they meet the minimum eligibility requirements.

They do not need to write any entrance exam.

Example (After 10th): A student wants to join a Certificate Course in Animation. The institute requires only a Class 10 pass certificate. If seats are available, the student can apply directly and join the course.

Example (After 12th): A student wants to join a Diploma in Fashion Design. The institute does not conduct an entrance exam and asks only for a class 12 pass certificate. They offer admission directly if seats are available.

Example:

  • A course has 30 seats available.
  • A student meets the eligibility criteria.
  • The institute does not conduct an entrance exam.
  • The student can be admitted directly to the course.
