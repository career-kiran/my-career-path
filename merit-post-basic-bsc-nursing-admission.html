Merit-Based Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Admission

Merit-Based Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Admission means students are selected based on their GNM marks, without writing an entrance examination.

Example: If 20 students apply for a Post Basic B.Sc Nursing course and the college has only 10 seats

The college may prepare a merit list based on the GNM marks of these 20 students.

The top 10 students with the highest marks in GNM will be offered admission.
