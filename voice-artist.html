Voice Artist

🧑‍💻 What Do They Do?

  • You might watch watched animated movies, dubbed films, advertisements, or listened to audiobooks
  • someone is using their voice to bring characters and stories to life.
  • These professionals are called Voice Artists.
  • 👉 In simple words: Voice Artists use their voice for dubbing, narration, animation, advertisements, gaming, and audio entertainment.

Where do Voice Artists work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in voice acting and audio performance.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🔵 Beginner Roles

📚

Audiobook Narrator

(After Certificate in Voice Culture and Modulation / Introduction to Voice Acting)

Reads books aloud for digital platforms using clear and expressive voice delivery.

🎙️

Junior Dubbing Artist

(After Diploma in Voice Over and Dubbing Techniques / Voice Technique Masterclass)

Gives voices to minor characters in movies, cartoons, and dubbed shows.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

📻

Radio Jockey (RJ) / Podcast Host

(After BA in Journalism and Mass Communication)

Hosts audio shows, interviews guests, and speaks directly to listeners.

💼

Corporate E-Learning Narrator

(After Diploma in Voice Over and Dubbing Techniques / Professional Voice-Over Certification)

Records voice-overs for company training videos and educational content.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🎬

Lead Character Voice Actor

(After BPA in Theatre / Acting / Character Voicing Performance)

Voices major characters in animated movies, games, and series.

📢

Commercial Voice-Over Specialist

(After MA in Mass Communication / PG Diploma in Radio Journalism)

Records voice-overs for television, internet ads, and brand campaigns.

🎬

Animation & Film Studios

They hire voice artists for animated movies, series, and dubbing projects.

🎮

Gaming Companies

They use voice actors for video game characters and storytelling.

🎙️

Podcast & Audio Platforms

They hire narrators, hosts, and audiobook voice artists.

📺

OTT & Streaming Platforms

They need dubbing artists for global movies and web series.
