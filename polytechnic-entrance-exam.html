State Polytechnic Entrance Exam

State Polytechnic Entrance Exams are conducted for admission to Diploma Engineering and Polytechnic courses after 10th class.

Each state in India conducts its own entrance examination.

It is conducted by the State Technical Education Department or State Board of Technical Education.

Through State Polytechnic Entrance Exams or state-level admission processes, students can get admission to various Diploma courses offered by Polytechnic colleges across India.

Courses include:-

  • All Diploma Engineering Courses
  • Technology and Technical Diploma Courses
  • Architecture and Design-related Diploma Courses
  • Agriculture and Allied Diploma Courses
  • Computer and IT-related Diploma Courses
  • Vocational and Skill-based Diploma Courses (available in some states)

Qualification: Passed or appearing in Class 10 (SSLC/Matriculation) from a recognized board.

Subjects: Mathematics, Science, and English are generally required. (Specific subject requirements may vary by state.)

Minimum Marks: Most states require a pass in Class 10.

Age: Age criteria vary by state.

Attempts: There is generally no limit on the number of attempts.

Mode: Usually Offline (Pen & Paper) or Computer-Based, depending on the state.

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Exam Frequency: Generally conducted once a year.

Duration: Varies by state.

Subjects: Mathematics, Science, and sometimes General Knowledge or English.

Total Questions: Varies by state.

Total Marks: Varies by state.

Marking Scheme: Determined by the respective state examination authority.

Mode: Online application

Official Websites:

  • Visit the official website of the respective State Polytechnic Entrance Exam or State Technical Education Board.

Steps to Apply:

  • Register: Create an account using your name, mobile number, and email ID.
  • Fill the Form: Enter personal, academic, and contact details.
  • Upload Documents: Upload photograph, signature, and required certificates.
  • Pay Fee: Pay the application fee online (UPI / Card / Net Banking).
  • Confirmation: Submit the application form and download the confirmation page.

Documents Required:

  • Passport-size photograph
  • Signature
  • Class 10 mark sheet / certificate
  • Valid ID proof
  • Domicile / category certificate (if applicable)

Note: Application procedures, fees, and required documents may vary by state. Always check the official notification before applying.
