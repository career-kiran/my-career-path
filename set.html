SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test)

SET is the entrance examination conducted by Symbiosis International (Deemed University) for admission to various undergraduate courses offered by its different institutes.

Students seeking admission to courses such as BBA, BCA, B.Sc Economics, Media Studies, and other undergraduate programmes at Symbiosis institutes may need to appear for SET.

Conducted by: Symbiosis International (Deemed University).

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Class 12 (10+2) pass or appearing from a recognized board.

Exam Pattern: Computer-Based Test (CBT) consisting of General English, Quantitative Aptitude, General Awareness, and Analytical & Logical Reasoning.

Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Total Questions: 60 Questions (may vary).

Total Marks: 60 Marks (may vary).

Frequency: Conducted once in a year with two test sessions(usually May).

Qualification:

  • Must have passed Class 12 (10+2) or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.
  • Students appearing for their Class 12 board examinations can also apply.
  • Students from any stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts/Humanities) may apply, depending on the course requirements.

Age:

  • There is no upper age limit for the SET examination.

Min mark:

  • General Category: Usually 50% marks in Class 12 (may vary depending on the institute and course).
  • SC/ST Category: Usually 45% marks in Class 12 (may vary depending on the institute and course).

Attempts:

  • There is generally no restriction on the number of attempts.
  • Candidates can apply again in future admission cycles if they meet the eligibility requirements.

Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Frequency: Conducted once in a year with two test sessions(usually in May).

Duration: 60 Minutes.

Total Questions: 60 Questions (may vary).

Total Marks: 60 Marks (may vary).

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Question Paper Structure:

  • General English: 16 Questions (16 Marks)
  • Quantitative Aptitude: 16 Questions (16 Marks)
  • General Awareness: 16 Questions (16 Marks)
  • Analytical & Logical Reasoning: 12 Questions (12 Marks)

Marking Scheme:

  • +1 Mark for every correct answer.
  • No Negative Marking: No marks are deducted for incorrect answers.

Important: Candidates can appear for one or both test sessions. If both sessions are attempted, the higher score is considered for the final percentile calculation.

Students who qualify the SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test) can apply for various undergraduate courses offered by participating Symbiosis institutes across India.

Courses may include:

  • Management & Commerce Courses
    • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
    • BBA in Accounting & Finance
    • BBA in Branding & Advertising
    • B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)
  • Computer & Technology Courses
    • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
    • B.Sc in Computer Science
    • B.Sc in Information Technology
  • Economics & Data Courses
    • B.Sc Economics
    • B.Sc Applied Statistics & Data Science
  • Media & Communication Courses
    • BA in Mass Communication
    • BA in Media Studies
    • BA in Journalism
  • Liberal Arts & Humanities Courses
    • BA Liberal Arts
    • BA English
    • BA Psychology
    • BA Sociology
    • BA Political Science
  • Design & Creative Courses
    • B.Des (at selected Symbiosis institutes)

Note: The courses available through SET may vary depending on the Symbiosis institute. Always check the official admission brochure for the latest list of courses and eligibility requirements.
