🇮🇳 Defence Services

🛡 What is Defense Service?

Defense Service refers to the armed forces of India that protect the nation from external threats and ensure national security.

🇮🇳 Types of Defense Forces in India

  • 🪖 Indian Army – Protects the land borders of the country and handles ground operations.
  • ⚓ Indian Navy – Protects the sea borders and ensures maritime security.
  • ✈️ Indian Air Force – Protects the airspace and handles aerial operations.

🛡️ What Do They Do?

  • Protect the country’s borders and airspace
  • Maintain national security
  • Participate in rescue and disaster relief operations
  • Handle emergency and war situations
  • Support peacekeeping missions

💖 You May Enjoy This Path If You:

  • are disciplined and physically active
  • like leadership and teamwork
  • are ready for challenging training
  • want an adventurous career

📌 Common Selection Process

  • 1) Written Exam: Candidates must first clear a written exam such as NDA, CDS, AFCAT, or other entry exams depending on the role.
  • 2) SSB Interview: Shortlisted candidates attend the 5-day Service Selection Board (SSB) interview. This stage tests personality, leadership, communication, and decision-making skills.
  • 3) Medical Examination: Candidates who clear SSB undergo a detailed medical test to ensure they meet required physical and medical standards.
  • 4) Final Merit List: Final selection is based on performance in written exam + SSB + medical fitness.
