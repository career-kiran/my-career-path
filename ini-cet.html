INI-CET (Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test)

What is INI-CET?

INI-CET is a national-level postgraduate medical entrance examination for students who have completed the MBBS course and wish to pursue postgraduate medical courses in India's top medical institutions.

Conducted by: AIIMS, New Delhi for admission to postgraduate courses in premier medical institutes like:

  • AIIMS New Delhi, PGIMER Chandigarh, JIPMER Puducherry, NIMHANS Bengaluru and SCTIMST Thiruvananthapuram.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: MBBS Degree

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 3 Hours

Total Questions: 200

Frequency: Twice a year (January and July)

Qualification: MBBS Degree from a recognised medical college.

Internship: Must have completed the compulsory internship as prescribed by AIIMS.

Registration: Must possess provisional or permanent medical registration.

Age: No upper age limit.

Attempts: No limit on the number of attempts.

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 3 Hours

Total Questions: 200 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Total Marks: 200 Marks

Correct Answer: +1 Mark

Incorrect Answer: -1/3 Mark

Unanswered Questions: 0 Marks

Questions are asked from various subjects covered during the MBBS course, including both pre-clinical and clinical subjects.

After clearing INI-CET, students can get admission to various postgraduate medical courses in Top institutes of India.

Courses include:-
  • MD (Doctor of Medicine)
  • MS (Master of Surgery)
  • DM (Doctorate of Medicine)
  • MCh (Magister Chirurgiae)
  • MDS (Master of Dental Surgery)
  • MD (Hospital Administration)

For registration, notifications and latest updates, visit the official AIIMS Exams website.
