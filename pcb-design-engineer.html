PCB Design Engineer

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Have you ever seen a green board inside a mobile phone, remote control, or any electronic devices?
  • This board helps all the electronic parts work together.
  • This green board is called a Printed Circuit Board (PCB).
  • The person who designs these boards is called a PCB Design Engineer.
  • 👉 In simple words: PCB Design Engineers design the circuit boards used inside electronic devices.

Where do PCB Design Engineers work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in PCB Design Engineering.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

📋

Junior PCB Layout Engineer

(0–2 Years Experience)

Designs basic PCB layouts using CAD software.

🧪

Hardware Test Engineer

(0–2 Years Experience)

Tests circuit boards and finds hardware issues.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

💻

PCB Design Engineer

(2–5 Years Experience)

Designs complex multi-layer circuit boards.

🔧

Hardware Design Engineer

(3–5 Years Experience)

Creates circuit designs for electronic products.

🟣 Advanced Roles

⚙️

Senior Hardware Architect

(5+ Years Experience)

Designs advanced electronic systems and boards.

🌍

VP of Hardware Systems

(8+ Years Experience)

Leads teams developing electronic products.
