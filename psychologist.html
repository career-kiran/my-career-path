Psychologist

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine a student who is very stressed and anxious.
  • He finds it difficult to focus on studies.
  • His parents take him to a person who can help him to overcome these problems.
  • That person talks to the student, identifies the real issue, and suggests ways to improve mental health and confidence.
  • These professionals are called Psychologists.
  • 👉 In simple words: Psychologists help people manage stress, emotions, anxiety, and mental health problems, so they can lead a healthier and happier life.

Where do Psychologists work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in psychology and mental health care.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

🤝

Mental Health Support Assistant

(After Certificate in Counseling / Basic Psychology Courses)

Provides emotional support and basic mental wellness guidance under supervision.

📝

Assistant Psychologist

(After B.A. / B.Sc. in Psychology)

Helps psychologists with case studies, observations, reports, and patient records.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

🏫

School Counselor

(After M.A. / M.Sc. in Psychology)

Supports students with academic stress, emotional problems, and behavioral issues.

🏢

Industrial-Organizational Consultant

(After M.A. / M.Sc. in Psychology)

Works with companies to improve employee well-being and workplace culture.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🏥

Licensed Clinical Psychologist

(After RCI-Approved M.A. / M.Phil. in Clinical Psychology)

Diagnoses mental disorders and provides advanced psychological therapy.

⚖️

Forensic Psychologist

(After Ph.D. / Advanced Clinical Psychology Specialization)

Studies criminal behavior and helps law enforcement agencies during investigations.

💻

Remote Telehealth Platforms

Provide online counseling and mental health support for international clients.

🌐

International Agencies & NGOs

Work on mental health awareness and emotional wellness programs worldwide.

🏢

Global Corporate Companies

Help employees manage stress, workplace pressure, and mental well-being.

🎓

International Universities

Work as researchers, counselors, or psychology lecturers in global institutions.
