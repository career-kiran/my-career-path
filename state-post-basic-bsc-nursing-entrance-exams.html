State Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams

What are State Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams?

State Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams are entrance examinations conducted by state authorities for admission to Post Basic B.Sc Nursing courses in India.

There is no single national entrance examination for admission to Post Basic B.Sc Nursing courses in India.

Conducted by: Various state authorities.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: GNM Nursing

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by state)

Duration: Varies by State

Questions: Varies by State

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

Qualification: General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) from a recognised institution.

Registration: Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife (RN/RM).

Minimum Marks: Minimum qualifying marks may vary by state.

Experience: Some states may require clinical work experience.

Domicile: Some states may require candidates to be residents of that state.

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by state)

Duration: Varies by State

Total Questions: Varies by State

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Marking Scheme: Varies by State
