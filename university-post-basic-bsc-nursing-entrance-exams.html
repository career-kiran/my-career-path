University Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams

What are University Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams?

University Post Basic B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exams are entrance examinations conducted by universities for admission to Post Basic B.Sc Nursing courses.

There is no single national entrance examination for admission to Post Basic B.Sc Nursing courses in India.

Conducted by: Various universities offering Post Basic B.Sc Nursing programmes.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: GNM Nursing

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by university)

Duration: Varies by University

Questions: Varies by University

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

Qualification: General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) from a recognised institution.

Registration: Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife (RN/RM).

Minimum Marks: Minimum qualifying marks may vary by university.

Experience: Some universities may require clinical work experience.

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by university)

Duration: Varies by University

Total Questions: Varies by University

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Marking Scheme: Varies by University
