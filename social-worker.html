Social Worker

Who is a Social worker?

A social worker is a professional who helps individuals,families and communities solve social and personal problems. They support people facing challengs like poverty,addiction,domestic issues,disability,mental health problems or lack of access to basic needs.

What does a social worker do?

  • Helps people get access to education,healthcare and government support
  • Supports children,women,elderly and vulnerable communities
  • Works with NGOs and government departments to improve social conditions

You may enjoy this career if you:

  • care deeply about helping people in difficult situations
  • Are patient and understanding
  • Want to create positive change in society
  • Want to work closely with communities

Real Life Examples

  • A social worker helps a child from a low-income family get admission to a school through government schemes
  • A social worker supports women facing domestic violence by guiding them to legal and emotional support services

Where do they work?

  • Case Worker – in NGOs
  • Community Development Officer – in Government Welfare Departments
  • Medical Social Worker – in Hospitals
  • Child Welfare Officer – in child protection services
  • Field Officer – in rural develpment projects

🌍 Global Opportunities

  • Community Support Worker – in International NGOs
  • Refugee Support officer – in UN(United Nations)agencies
  • Social Services Officer – in public welfare departments abroad

Important Note

This career requires emotional strength and patience. You will work with people who are facing real-life struggles. If you truly care about improving lives and are ready to support others during difficult times,this can be a very meanigful career.
