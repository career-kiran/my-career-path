🖊️ Author

🧑‍💻 What Do They Do?

  • Authors write books and stories
  • They create characters and ideas
  • Some write fiction
  • Some write real-life topics
  • They edit and improve their writing
  • 👉 In simple words: Authors create books and stories for people to read.
