🖊️ Author / Writer

🧠 Imagine This...

"Imagine a person sitting in a quiet place with a notebook or laptop.

An idea for a story, article, or blog comes to the mind.

He/She start writing their thoughts into words.

Slowly, those words become a full story, a blog post, or even a book.

People around the world begin reading what they wrote.

🛡️ This is the work of an Author or Writer.

Authors and writers use words to tell stories, share ideas, and communicate information through books, articles, blogs, and other written content."

💖 You may enjoy this job if You...

  • Enjoy writing stories, articles, or blogs.
  • Like expressing your thoughts and ideas through words.
  • Enjoy reading books, stories, or online content.

🔧 Want to Try This Yourself?

  • Try writing a short story or a simple article about a topic you enjoy.
  • Read different books, blogs, or articles and observe how writers structure their ideas.
  • Practice writing small paragraphs about your daily experiences or interesting topics.
  • Use writing tools like Google Docs or Notion to organize your ideas and drafts.
  • Try AI writing tools like ChatGPT or Grammarly to improve your writing and get suggestions.

🎓 How to Become an Author / Writer

After 10th Standard (Foundation & Creative Skills)

Courses:

  • Certificate in Creative Writing
  • Diploma in Creative Writing (English / Hindi)
  • Diploma in Journalism

Duration: 3 – 6 months

Eligibility: 10th pass from any recognized board

Minimum Marks: Usually 45% – 50% aggregate

Age Limit: Around 15 years minimum

Exam to Pass: Usually direct admission or a basic language proficiency test in some private institutes

After 12th Standard (Undergraduate Degrees)

Courses:

  • B.A. in English Literature
  • B.A. in Journalism & Mass Communication
  • B.A. in Creative Writing

Duration: Around 3 – 4 years

Eligibility: 10+2 pass in any stream (Arts / Humanities preferred)

Minimum Marks: Usually around 50% in 12th

Age Limit: Around 17 – 25 years for some universities

Exam to Pass:

  • National Level: CUET-UG
  • Private Universities: SET (Symbiosis), JSAT (Jindal Global), or university-specific entrance tests

After Graduation (Advanced Specialisation)

Courses:

  • M.A. in Creative Writing
  • M.A. in English Literature
  • PG Diploma in Creative Writing

Duration: 1 – 2 years

Eligibility: Graduation in any field (English / Arts background is helpful)

Minimum Marks: Around 50% in graduation

Age Limit: Usually no upper age limit

Exam to Pass:

  • National Level: CUET-PG
  • Some institutes may require writing samples or interviews

(Note: Age limits and minimum marks may vary depending on the institute, exam authority, or university regulations.)

⭐ Note

Unlike many other careers, becoming an

Author or Writer does not always require a specific degree.

Many successful writers build their careers through practice, creativity, and publishing their work.

🏢 Where do Authors / Writers Work?

  • Book Author – work with publishing houses to write novels, biographies, and non-fiction books.
  • Content Writer – work in content writing agencies, startups, and digital marketing companies.
  • Journalist / Article Writer – work in newspapers, magazines, and online news platforms.
  • Copywriter – work in advertising agencies creating slogans, ads, and campaign content.
  • Script Writer – work in film, television, and web series production companies.
  • Blog Writer – write articles for websites, blogs, and online media platforms.
  • Freelance Writer – work independently for publishers, companies, and online platforms.

🌍 Global Opportunities

  • International Publishing Houses – publish books and novels for readers around the world.
  • Global Media Companies – write articles, reports, and digital content for international audiences.
  • International Advertising Agencies – create marketing content and campaign scripts for global brands.
  • Film & Television Industry – write scripts and stories for international movies and shows.
  • Online Content Platforms – write blogs and articles for global websites and digital platforms.
  • Freelance International Projects – work remotely with clients and publishers from different countries.

⭐ Career Insight

Many writers start by writing blogs, articles, or short stories.

With practice, they may work as content writers, journalists, or script writers.

Some writers publish books, while others work freelance or with media companies.

Regular writing and improving your skills are very important in this career.
