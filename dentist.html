Dentist

A dentist helps people take care of their teeth, gums, and mouth. They treat dental problems, relieve pain, and guide patients on maintaining good oral health.

You may enjoy this career if you…

  • Like working closely with people
  • Are comfortable doing detailed, hands-on work
  • Have patience and good focus
  • Enjoy focused,practical work that requires care and precision

Course to become a Dentist (India)

Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

Duration: 5 years (4 years study + 1 year internship)

Eligibility:

  • 12th standard with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum marks: 50% (General category)

Entrance exam: NEET-UG

NEET-UG NEET-UG is the national-level entrance exam required for admission to BDS in government and private dental colleges in India.

Master of Dental Surgery (MDS)

Duration: 3 years

Eligibility:

  • Completed BDS
  • Compulsory rotating internship completed

Entrance exam: NEET-MDS

Focus of this course:
Advanced training in specialities such as Orthodontics, Oral Surgery, Periodontics, Prosthodontics, Public Health Dentistry, and others.

Where do Dentists work?

  • Dental clinics and hospitals
  • Government health services
  • Private practice
  • Dental colleges and teaching institutions

Important note

Dentistry requires consistent practice, precision, and good communication with patients. It suits those who enjoy hands-on clinical work and long-term patient care.
