Performance Video Submission

Performance Video Submission is a selection process conducted by some colleges, universities, and performing arts institutes for admission to performing arts courses.

In this process, students record a video of their performance and submit it to the institute for evaluation.

Students are not performing directly infront of the judges.

The video may include:

• Singing a song
• Playing a musical instrument
• Performing a dance
• Acting a short scene
• Any other performance asked by the institute

Example:

A student applies for a Bachelor of Dance course.

The institute asks the student to record a dance performance and upload the video.

The judges watch the video and check the student's skills, confidence, and overall performance.

Based on the video, the institute decides whether to offer admission.
