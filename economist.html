Economist

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine the government wants to build a new public park in the city.
  • But they have only a limited amount of money.
  • Economists study the situation and help the government decide how the money should be used wisely.
  • They may suggest whether the money should be used for parks, hospitals, schools, or roads.
  • 👉 In simple words: Economists help governments and companies make smart decisions about money, prices, and public services.

🎓 How to Become an Economist

These Economics programs help students learn about money, business, prices, banking, markets, trade, and how the economy works.

B.A. / B.A. (Hons.) in Economics

Duration: 3 – 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass (Any stream — Arts, Science, or Commerce; Mathematics preferred by top colleges)

Entrance Exam: CUET UG / University Merit-Based

B.Sc. / B.Sc. (Hons.) in Economics

Duration: 3 – 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass (Science or Commerce stream with strong Mathematics background)

Entrance Exam: CUET UG / University Entrance Exam

Integrated B.A. + M.A. / B.Sc. + M.Sc. in Economics

Duration: 5 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass (Strong focus on Mathematics or Statistics)

Entrance Exam: National or University-Level Entrance Exam

Where do Economists work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in Economics and financial research.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

📊

Economic Research Assistant

(After B.A. / B.Sc. in Economics)

Collects economic data and prepares reports.

📈

Junior Data Analyst

(After B.A. / B.Sc. in Economics / Statistics)

Studies market trends and business data.

💰

Financial Risk Trainee

(After B.A. / B.Sc. in Economics / Finance)

Helps companies understand financial risks.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

🏦

Financial Analyst

(After M.A. / M.Sc. in Economics)

Analyzes financial trends and investments.

🛒

Market Research Analyst

(After M.A. / M.Sc. in Economics / Marketing)

Studies customer demand and market behavior.

🏛️

Policy Analyst

(After M.A. / M.Sc. in Economics / Public Policy)

Studies government programs and policies.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🌍

Chief Economist

(After Ph.D. / Extensive Experience)

Advises companies and governments on the economy.

📉

Quantitative Economist

(After Ph.D. / M.S. in Quantitative Economics)

Builds models to predict market changes.

💼

Consultant Economist

(After Ph.D. / Advanced Master’s)

Gives economic advice to global companies.

🏦

International Financial Institutions

Work with global organizations like the World Bank and IMF.

📊

Global Research Organizations

Study economic trends and policies for international research groups.

🌐

Multinational Companies

Help global companies understand markets and business growth.

💹

International Investment Firms

Analyze global markets and investment opportunities.
