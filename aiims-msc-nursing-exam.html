AIIMS M.Sc Nursing Entrance Examination

What is AIIMS M.Sc Nursing Entrance Examination?

AIIMS M.Sc Nursing Entrance Examination is a national-level entrance examination conducted for admission to M.Sc Nursing programmes offered by AIIMS institutes in India.

Conducted by: All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 90 Minutes

Questions: 90 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

Qualification: B.Sc Nursing, B.Sc (Hons.) Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing from a recognised institution.

Registration: Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife (RN/RM).

Minimum Marks:

  • 60% - General / OBC Category
  • 55% - SC / ST Category

Experience: Some courses may require clinical work experience.

Nationality: Indian Citizen.

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 90 Minutes

Total Questions: 90 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Total Marks: 90 Marks

Correct Answer: +1 Mark

Incorrect Answer: -1/3 Mark

Unanswered Questions: 0 Marks

Questions are generally asked from the subjects studied during the B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing programme.

After clearing the AIIMS M.Sc Nursing Entrance Examination, students can get admission to M.Sc Nursing programmes offered by AIIMS institutes.
