State Level ITI Selection

State Level ITI Selection is the selection process for getting admission to Industrial Training Institute (ITI) courses.

students do not have to write an entrance exam.

Students are getting admission based on their Class 8, Class 10 or Class 12 marks.(depends on the course)

Example:

Imagine in a state, 100 seats are available for the Electrician course, and 500 students apply for admission.

Based on the 10th mark, the state prepares a merit list of these 500 students.

The 100 students with the highest marks are offered admission to the Electrician course.
