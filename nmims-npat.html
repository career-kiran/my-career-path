NMIMS NPAT (National Test for Programs After Twelfth)

NMIMS NPAT is a university-level entrance exam for admission to various undergraduate programmes.

NPAT scores are accepted for admission to undergraduate programmes such as BBA, B.Com, B.Sc. Finance, B.Sc. Economics, BBA Branding & Advertising, BBA International Business, Liberal Arts, and several other courses offered by NMIMS.

Conducted by: SVKM's Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS).

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Class 12 or equivalent examination from a recognized board.

Exam Pattern: Computer-Based Test (CBT) consisting of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Mode: Online (Computer-Based Test).

Total Questions: 120 Questions.

Frequency: Conducted once a year.

Qualification:

  • Must have passed or be appearing for the Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent examination from a recognized board.
  • Candidates appearing for their Class 12 board examinations are also eligible to apply, provided they meet the admission requirements after the results are declared.

Age:

  • Candidates should generally be 25 years of age or below at the time of admission.

Minimum Marks:

  • For the Exam: There is no minimum percentage required to appear for the NMIMS NPAT examination.
  • For Admission: The minimum Class 12 marks required vary depending on the programme. Some programmes also require Mathematics or Statistics as a compulsory subject.

Attempts:

  • You can take the NMIMS NPAT examination up to 3 times (1 first attempt + 2 retakes) during the admission year.
  • Note: The highest score among all your attempts is considered for preparing the merit list.

Nationality:

  • Indian, NRI, and foreign candidates can apply, subject to the admission rules of NMIMS.

Mode of Examination: Online (Computer-Based Test).

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Duration: 100 Minutes (1 Hour 40 Minutes).

Frequency: Conducted once a year.

Total Questions: 120 Questions.

Total Marks: 120 Marks.

Sections:

  • Quantitative & Numerical Ability: 40 Questions (40 Marks).
  • Reasoning & General Intelligence: 40 Questions (40 Marks).
  • Proficiency in English: 40 Questions (40 Marks).

Marking Scheme:

  • Correct Answer: +1 Mark.
  • Incorrect Answer: 0 Marks (No negative marking).
  • Unattempted Question: 0 Marks.

Students who qualify through the NMIMS NPAT (National Test for Programs After Twelfth) can apply for various undergraduate programmes offered by NMIMS across its campuses.

Undergraduate Courses:

  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)
  • B.Sc. Finance
  • B.Sc. Economics
  • BBA (FinTech)
  • Integrated BBA–MBA
  • BBA (Branding & Advertising)
  • BBA (International Business)
  • BBA (Management & Marketing)
  • BBA (Finance)
  • B.A. (Liberal Arts)

Important Note:

Qualifying for the NMIMS NPAT does not guarantee admission.

Candidates must meet the eligibility criteria of the respective programme and campus.

Admission is based on the NMIMS NPAT score, eligibility criteria, and the admission process prescribed by NMIMS for the chosen programme.
