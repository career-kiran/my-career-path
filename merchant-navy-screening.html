Merchant Navy Screening

Merchant Navy Screening is a selection process conducted by some maritime institutes and shipping companies for admission to Merchant Navy courses and training programmes.

The screening is conducted to check whether a student is suitable for a career in the Merchant Navy.

Screening may include:

  • Aptitude Test
  • Personal Interview
  • Medical Examination
  • Physical Fitness Assessment
  • Eyesight and Colour Vision Test
  • Document Verification

A student completes the screening process and receives the final selection result.

Based on the student's performance and medical fitness, eligible candidates may receive admission or sponsorship for Merchant Navy courses.

Conducted by: Maritime institutes, shipping companies, or the respective admission authority.
