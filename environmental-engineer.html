Environmental Engineer

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Every day, we use water for drinking, cooking, and bathing.
  • But water from rivers and lakes is not always clean and safe.
  • Special systems are used to remove dirt, chemicals, and harmful substances from the water.
  • The person who designs and manages these systems is called an Environmental Engineer. 🌱
  • 👉 In simple words: Environmental Engineers help keep our water, air, and environment clean and safe.
