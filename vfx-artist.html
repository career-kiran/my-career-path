🪄 VFX Artist

🧑‍🏭 What Do They Do?

  • When you watch a superhero or fantasy movie
  • Many scenes like explosions, magical effects, characters, backgrounds etc are created using computer-generated effects and digital graphics.
  • The person who does this is called a VFX Artist.
    • 👉 In simple words: VFX Artists use computers and software to create amazing visual effects for movies and videos.

Where do VFX Artists work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some places where VFX Artists can work.

You can start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢Beginner Roles

✂️

Roto Artist

Trace live-action footage frame-by-frame to create masks for compositing.

🧹

Paint / Prep Artist

Remove unwanted objects like wires, rigs, and markers from footage.

🎯

Matchmove Artist

Track camera movement so 3D elements fit perfectly into scenes.

🔵Mid-Level Roles

🖥️

Compositor

Blend digital effects with live-action footage to create final scenes.

🔥

FX Artist

Create effects like fire, smoke, explosions, water, and destruction.

🏞️

Environment TD

Build and light digital backgrounds and 3D environments.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🎬

VFX Supervisor

Lead the creative vision and manage the VFX production pipeline.

💡

Lighting Lead

Oversee lighting teams to match digital scenes with real footage.

🌌

CG Supervisor

Manage 3D asset creation and maintain the project’s visual quality.

🎬

Hollywood & International Films

You create visual effects for superhero, fantasy, and sci-fi movies.

🎮

AAA Game Studios

Design explosions, magic effects, environments for international video games.

📺

OTT & Streaming Platforms

You work on Netflix, Disney+, and Amazon Prime series.

🪐

Virtual Production Studios

Create digital worlds using Unreal Engine and LED wall technology.

🌍

International Advertising Agencies

You create cinematic visual effects for commercials, brand campaign.
