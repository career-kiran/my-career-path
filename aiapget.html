AIAPGET (All India AYUSH Post Graduate Entrance Test)

What is AIAPGET?

AIAPGET is a national-level postgraduate entrance examination for students who have completed:

BAMS, BHMS, BUMS or BSMS

and wish to pursue postgraduate education in Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Unani and Siddha systems of medicine.

Conducted by: National Testing Agency (NTA).

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: BAMS, BHMS, BUMS or BSMS Degree

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 2 Hours

Total Questions: 120

Frequency: Once a year (usually in July)

Qualification: BAMS, BHMS, BUMS or BSMS Degree from a recognised medical college.

Internship: Must have completed the compulsory rotatory internship.

Registration: Must possess provisional or permanent registration with the relevant AYUSH council.

Age: No upper age limit.

Attempts: No limit on the number of attempts.

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 2 Hours

Total Questions: 120 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Total Marks: 480 Marks

Correct Answer: +4 Marks

Incorrect Answer: -1 Mark

Unanswered Questions: 0 Marks

Questions are asked from subjects studied during the BAMS, BHMS, BUMS or BSMS course.

After clearing AIAPGET, students can get admission to various postgraduate AYUSH courses in India.

Courses include:-
  • MD (Ayurveda)
  • MS (Ayurveda)
  • MD (Homoeopathy)
  • MD (Unani)
  • MS (Unani)
  • MD (Siddha)
  • For registration, notifications and latest updates, visit the official AIAPGET website.
  • For counselling, seat allotment and admission process, visit the AACCC website.
