SRFTI Entrance Test

SRFTI Entrance Test is an entrance examination conducted by the Satyajit Ray Film & Television Institute (SRFTI) for admission to various film, television, animation, and media programmes.

The entrance test is conducted to assess a candidate's knowledge, aptitude, creativity, and suitability for the chosen course.

Depending on the programme, shortlisted candidates may also have to appear for additional selection stages such as an audition, practical test, portfolio review, or personal interview.

Conducted by: Satyajit Ray Film & Television Institute (SRFTI), Kolkata.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Varies depending on the course. Candidates must meet the eligibility criteria prescribed by SRFTI.

Exam Pattern: Includes a Preliminary Selection Test (PST). Shortlisted candidates may also have to appear for an audition, practical test, portfolio review, or personal interview, depending on the course.

Mode: Offline (Pen-and-Paper).

Total Questions: 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Frequency: Conducted once a year.

Important: The admission process and selection stages vary depending on the course. Always check the official SRFTI admission notification for the latest information.

Qualification:

  • The educational qualification required varies depending on the course.
  • Candidates must have passed the required examination from a recognized board or university.
  • Always check the eligibility criteria for the specific programme you are applying for.

Age:

  • There is generally no upper age limit for most SRFTI programmes.
  • However, age requirements may vary for certain courses.

Minimum Marks:

  • For the Exam: Candidates must meet the minimum marks prescribed for the chosen programme.
  • For Admission: Final admission depends on your performance in the selection process and fulfilment of all eligibility criteria.

Attempts:

  • There is generally no limit on the number of attempts.

Nationality:

  • Indian citizens are eligible to apply.
  • Foreign nationals may also apply as per SRFTI admission guidelines.

Mode of Examination: Offline (Pen-and-Paper) for the Preliminary Selection Test (PST).

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Duration: 2 Hours.

Frequency: Conducted once a year.

Total Questions: 100 Questions.

Total Marks: 100 Marks.

Structure: The test is divided into two parts:

  • Part A: General Aptitude Test (50 Marks).
  • Part B: Common Subject Area Test (50 Marks).

Marking Scheme:

  • Correct Answer: 1 Mark is awarded for each correct answer.
  • Incorrect Answer: Follow the instructions mentioned in the official examination guidelines.

Important: Candidates who qualify in the Preliminary Selection Test (PST) are shortlisted for the Final Selection Test, which may include practical tests, portfolio reviews, auditions, or personal interviews, depending on the course.

Students who qualify in the SRFTI Entrance Test can apply for various programmes offered by the Satyajit Ray Film & Television Institute (SRFTI).

Available Programmes (MFA in Cinema):

  • Direction & Screenplay Writing
  • Cinematography
  • Editing
  • Sound Recording & Design
  • Producing for Film & Television
  • Animation Cinema

Available Programmes (MFA in Electronic & Digital Media):

  • Writing
  • Management
  • Cinematography
  • Direction & Producing
  • Editing
  • Sound

Other Programmes:

  • Screen Acting
  • Film Preservation & Restoration

Important Note:

Qualifying in the SRFTI Entrance Test does not guarantee admission.

Final admission depends on your performance in the Preliminary Selection Test (PST), Final Selection Test, fulfilment of the eligibility criteria, and seat availability.
