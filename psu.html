🏭 What are PSU Jobs?

Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) are government-owned companies that operate in sectors like energy, oil, engineering, banking, and infrastructure.

🏢 List of PSU Companies

  • ONGC – Oil and Natural Gas Corporation
  • BHEL – Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited
  • NTPC – National Thermal Power Corporation
  • SAIL – Steel Authority of India Limited
  • GAIL – Gas Authority of India Limited
  • IOCL – Indian Oil Corporation Limited
  • HPCL – Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited
  • BEL – Bharat Electronics Limited

Note: These are some examples. There are many other PSU companies across different sectors.

🛡️ What Do They Do?

  • Work in government-owned companies
  • Handle technical, office, and management work
  • Maintain equipment and systems
  • Manage projects and daily operations

💖 You May Enjoy This Path If You:

  • prefer stable, professional work environments
  • are interested in engineering, management, or finance
  • want long-term growth in government-owned companies

📌 How to Get Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) Jobs?

You can get PSU jobs through recruitment exams, GATE scores, or direct recruitment notifications released by individual PSUs.

🎓 Jobs After 10th

Helper / Technician Assistant (Entry Level)

Eligibility: 10th pass

Age Limit: 18 – 25 years (varies by PSU)

Selection: PSU recruitment notification / Trade test

Technician Assistant: They support maintenance work, and handle basic operational tasks.

ℹ Note:Recruitment notification - means an official job announcement released by a PSU on its website. It includes details about eligibility, age limit, selection process, and application dates.

🎓 Jobs After 12th

Junior Assistant / Clerk

Eligibility: 12th pass

Age Limit: 18 – 27 years (varies by PSU)

Selection: PSU recruitment notification / Written test

Technician / Trade Apprentice

Eligibility: 12th pass (Science) or ITI

Age Limit: 18 – 27 years (varies by PSU)

Selection: Written test / Trade test / Apprenticeship recruitment

Junior Assistant: Handle office work, documentation, and support administrative tasks.

Technician: Assist in technical operations, maintenance, and production work.

🎓 Jobs After Graduation

Graduate Engineer Trainee (GET)

Eligibility: B.E / B.Tech (relevant engineering stream)

Age Limit: Usually up to 27 – 30 years (varies by PSU)

Selection: GATE score / PSU recruitment exam

Management Trainee (HR / Finance / Marketing)

Eligibility: Graduation + MBA / relevant qualification

Age Limit: Varies by PSU

Selection: PSU recruitment exam / Interview

Accounts / Administrative Officer

Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree (Commerce / relevant field)

Age Limit: Varies by PSU

Selection: PSU recruitment exam / Direct recruitment

Graduate Engineer Trainee: Work in engineering, production, maintenance, and project roles.

Management Trainee: Handle management, HR, finance, or business operations.

Administrative Officer: Manage accounts, documentation, compliance, and office administration.

📝 Exam Full Forms

  • GATE – Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering
  • GET – Graduate Engineer Trainee
  • MBA – Master of Business Administration
  • ITI – Industrial Training Institute

📌 Selection Process

The selection process for PSU jobs may vary depending on the company and post. It generally includes the following stages:

  • 1) Written Exam / GATE Score: Some PSUs recruit through their own exam or through GATE scores.
  • 2) Shortlisting: Candidates are shortlisted based on exam score or academic performance.
  • 3) Interview / Group Discussion: Evaluation of technical knowledge, communication, and problem-solving skills.
  • 4) Document Verification: Verification of educational and identity documents.
  • 5) Medical Examination: Basic health check to ensure fitness for the role.
