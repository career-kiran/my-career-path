Hair Stylist

💇 What Do They Do?

  • When you want a hair cut or hairstyle, you visit a salon or barber shop....rite?
  • There someone carefully cuts, styles, washes, and sets the hair to make it look neat and stylish.
  • That person is called a Hair Stylist.
  • 👉 In simple words: Hair Stylists cut and style hair to help people look stylish and confident.

🎓 How to Become a Doctor

These Hair Stylist and Hair Dressing programs help students learn hair cutting, hairstyling, hair coloring, grooming, salon management, and professional hair care skills.

ITI in Cosmetology (NCVT/SCVT)

Duration: 1 Year (2 Semesters)

Eligibility: 10th Pass

Entrance Exam: State-Level ITI Merit Selection (Based on 10th Board Marks) / Delhi CET

Diploma in Hair Dressing and Styling (Polytechnic)

Duration: 1 – 2 Years

Eligibility: 10th Pass

Entrance Exam: State Polytechnic Entrance Exams

Advanced Diploma in Hair Styling and Salon Management

Duration: 1 Year

Eligibility: 10th Pass or above

Entrance Exam: Direct institutional selection based on 10th board marks
