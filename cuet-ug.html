CUET UG (Common University Entrance Test - Undergraduate)

CUET UG is a common admission test used by many universities in India.

A student writes the CUET UG examination and receives a score.

The student can use this score to apply for various courses in universities that accept CUET scores for admission.

Courses may include: B.Sc, B.A., B.Com, BBA, BCA, Agriculture, Arts, Science, Commerce and many other undergraduate programmes.

Conducted by: National Testing Agency (NTA).

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: 12th Pass

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Questions: Varies based on the subjects chosen

Frequency: Conducted once a year(usually May)

Qualification: Passed or appearing in Class 12 from a recognised board.

Appearing = currently in Class 12 (writing exams this year)

Age: No age limit

Subjects: Subject requirements may vary depending on the course and university.

Minimum Marks:

  • Minimum marks requirements may vary according to the course and university.

Attempts: No limit on the number of attempts.

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Exam Frequency: Conducted once a year

Duration: Varies depending on the subjects chosen

Total Questions: Varies depending on the subjects chosen

Total Marks: Varies depending on the subjects chosen

Marking Scheme:

  • +5 - for every correct answer
  • -1 - for every wrong answer
  • 0 - for unattempted questions

CUET UG scores may be used for admission to various undergraduate courses offered by universities that accept CUET scores.

Courses may include:
  • B.Sc Courses
  • B.A. Courses

  • BBA
