XOET (XIC Online Entrance Test)

XOET is the entrance examination conducted by Xavier Institute of Communications (XIC), Mumbai, for admission to its professional Postgraduate Diploma courses in fields such as Journalism, Advertising, Public Relations, and Digital Media.

Note: This exam is specific to XIC, Mumbai, and is not offered by other institutions with the Xavier name.

Conducted by: Xavier Institute of Communications (XIC), Mumbai.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

Exam Pattern: Includes objective sections and subjective sections.

Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Total Questions: Varies by course.

Total Marks: Typically 100 Marks (may vary).

Frequency: Conducted once a year (usually in April).

Selection Process: Shortlisted candidates must appear for an online personal interview.

Qualification:

  • Must have completed a Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
  • Students currently in the final year of their graduation can also apply.
  • Candidates from any stream (Arts, Commerce, Science, Engineering, Management, Hospitality, and related fields) may apply.

Age:

  • Candidates must be 35 years of age or below.

Minimum Marks:

  • There is no specific minimum percentage requirement mentioned for the qualifying degree.

Attempts:

  • There is no limit on the number of attempts, provided the candidate continues to meet the eligibility requirements.

Nationality:

  • Candidates must be Indian nationals.

Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Frequency: Conducted once a year (usually in April).

Duration: Typically 60–90 minutes.

Total Marks: 100 Marks.

Marking Scheme:

  • Correct Answer: +1 mark is awarded for every correct answer.
  • Incorrect Answer: There is no negative marking; no marks are deducted for incorrect answers.

Question Type: A blend of Objective (MCQs) and Descriptive (Subjective) questions.

Question Paper Structure:

  • Objective Section: General Knowledge, Media Awareness, Language Skills, and Logical/Analytical Reasoning.
  • Subjective Section: Descriptive writing tasks testing creativity, opinion, and social values.

Selection Process:

  • Stage 1: XOET (XIC Online Entrance Test).
  • Stage 2: Personal Interview for shortlisted candidates.

Important: Candidates must qualify in the XOET examination to be considered for the interview stage.

Students who qualify through the XOET admission process can apply for various 11-month full-time Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) courses offered by the Xavier Institute of Communications (XIC), Mumbai.

Available Postgraduate Diploma Courses:

  • Advertising, Marketing Communications & Customer Analytics
  • Applied Creativity for Advertising & Marketing Communications
  • Event Planning & Experiential Marketing
  • Film, Television & Digital Media
  • Journalism & Media Convergence
  • Mass Communication & Digital Media
  • Public Relations & Corporate Communication

Other Learning Opportunities at XIC:

  • Career Advancement Programmes (CAP)
    • Strategic Integrated Communication Programme (SICP)
    • Strategic Media & Digital Communication (SMDC)
  • Certificate Courses & Workshops
    • Public Speaking & Personality Enhancement
    • Interview Skills & Confidence Building
    • Creative Writing
    • Script Writing for Media
    • Photography (Basic to Advanced)
    • Broadcast & Voicing Masterclass
    • Radio Jockeying
    • Neuromarketing & Consumer Psychology
    • Social Media Marketing & Analytics

Important Note:

  • The Postgraduate Diploma courses are designed for graduates or final-year graduation students from any stream.
  • Career Advancement Programmes (CAP), Certificate Courses, and Workshops may have separate admission requirements and application processes.
