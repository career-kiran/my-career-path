Aptitude Test

Aptitude Test is a selection process conducted by some colleges, universities, and music institutes for admission to instrumental music courses.

This test is mainly conducted to check whether a student has the ability to learn and play a musical instrument.

The test may include:

• Listening to different musical sounds
• Identifying high and low notes
• Matching the sound played by the examiner
• Answering a few simple questions about music

In simple words, this test is conducted to find out whether a student has the basic skills needed to learn a musical instrument.

Example:

A student applies for a Bachelor of Instrumental Music.

During the test, the student listens to different musical sounds, repeats simple rhythms, and answers a few easy questions.

The judges check the student's musical ability and decide whether to offer admission.
