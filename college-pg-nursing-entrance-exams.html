College PG Nursing Entrance Exams

What are College PG Nursing Entrance Exams?

College PG Nursing Entrance Exams are entrance examinations conducted by individual nursing colleges and institutions for admission to postgraduate nursing courses in India.

There is no single admission process followed by all colleges in India.

Conducted by: Individual nursing colleges and institutions.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by college)

Duration: Varies by College

Questions: Varies by College

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

Qualification: B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing from a recognised institution.

Registration: Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife (RN/RM).

Experience: Some colleges may require clinical work experience.

Minimum Marks: Minimum qualifying marks may vary by college.

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by college)

Duration: Varies by College

Total Questions: Varies by College

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Subjects: Nursing subjects (may vary by college)

Marking Scheme: Varies by College
