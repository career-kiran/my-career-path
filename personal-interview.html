Personal Interview

Personal Interview is a process in which a student has a short conversation with a teacher, professor, or admission panel.

During the interview, students may be asked questions about their education, interests, goals, and reasons for choosing the course.

Example :

    Imagine a student going to take admission for a Bachelor of Design (B.Des.) programme.

    College /University may take an interview before admission.

    The interviewer may ask questions such as "Why do you want to study design?" or "Which type of design interests you the most?"

  • The type of questions asked during a personal interview depends on the course or programme you have chosen.
