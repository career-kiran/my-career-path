FEAT (FLAME Entrance Aptitude Test)

FEAT is the entrance examination conducted by FLAME University for admission to its undergraduate courses.

The test evaluates a student's verbal ability, reasoning skills, quantitative aptitude, and general knowledge.

Conducted by: FLAME University, Pune.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Class 12 (10+2) pass or appearing from any recognized board.

Exam Pattern: Aptitude test covering Verbal Ability, Reasoning Ability, Quantitative Ability, and General Knowledge.

Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Total Questions: Typically 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Total Marks: Varies by course.

Frequency: Conducted multiple times during the admission cycle.

Important: Admission is holistic. Candidates must also complete an on-the-spot essay and a personal interview.

Qualification:

  • Must have passed Class 12 (10+2) or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.
  • Students currently appearing for their Class 12 board examinations may also apply.
  • Students from any stream (Science, Commerce, or Arts/Humanities) may apply.

Age:

  • Applicants must be less than 21 years of age

Minimum Marks:

  • Candidates must have secured an aggregate of at least 45% in their Class 12 or equivalent examination.

Attempts:

  • There is no restriction on the number of attempts.
  • Candidates are eligible to apply in future admission cycles if they meet the necessary requirements.

Mode: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT).

Frequency: Conducted multiple times during the admission cycle.

Duration: Usually 120 Minutes.

Total Questions: Typically 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Total Marks: Varies by course.

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).

Question Paper Structure:

  • Verbal Ability: Tests reading comprehension, vocabulary, grammar, and language skills.
  • Reasoning Ability: Tests logical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Quantitative Ability: Tests basic mathematics and numerical aptitude.
  • General Knowledge: Tests awareness of current affairs and general topics.

Selection Process:

  • Stage 1: FEAT (FLAME Entrance Aptitude Test).
  • Stage 2: On-the-Spot Essay(20 minutes).
  • Stage 3: Personal Interview.

Evaluation:

  • Performance in the FEAT, Essay, and Personal Interview is considered for admission.
  • The final selection is based on the overall performance of the candidate.

Important: FLAME University follows a holistic admission process and evaluates candidates performance in all stages.

Students qualifying through the FEAT admission process can apply for various undergraduate courses at FLAME University. The university follows a Liberal Education model, allowing students to combine majors and minors across different disciplines.
