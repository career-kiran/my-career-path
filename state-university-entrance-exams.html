State University Entrance Exams

State University Entrance Exams are examinations conducted by State Government Universities for admission to various undergraduate, postgraduate, professional, and research programmes.

These examinations may be conducted for programmes in science, arts, commerce, management, law, education, engineering, and research fields.

Conducted by: Individual State Universities.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Varies by Course and State

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by state)

Duration: Varies by State

Questions: Varies by State

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

State University Entrance Exams may be used for admission to various undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional courses.

Courses available may vary from university to university and state to state.

Courses may include:

  • Engineering & Technology (B.E. / B.Tech / M.Tech)
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm / M.Pharm / D.Pharm)
  • Architecture & Planning (B.Arch / B.Plan)
  • Nursing & Allied Health Sciences (B.Sc Nursing / BPT / BMLT)
  • Agriculture & Forestry (B.Sc. Agriculture / Horticulture / Sericulture)
  • Veterinary Science & Animal Husbandry (B.V.Sc & AH)
  • Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences (B.A. / M.A. / MSW)
  • Commerce, Finance & Banking (B.Com / M.Com)
  • Business Administration & Management (BBA / MBA / BMS)
  • Computer Applications & IT (BCA / MCA)
  • Law (Integrated 5-Year LL.B / 3-Year LL.B / LL.M)
  • Education & Teacher Training (B.Ed / M.Ed / B.P.Ed)
  • Hotel Management & Catering Technology (BHMCT / MHMCT)
  • Fine Arts, Design & Media (BFA / B.Des / BJMC)
