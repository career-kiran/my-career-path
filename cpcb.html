🌿 Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB)

🌿 What is CPCB?

The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) is India’s main pollution control authority.

It works under the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.

It checks pollution levels and make rules to keep our air, water, and environment safe.

🛡️ What Do They Do?

  • Check quality of air across cities and industrial areas.
  • Test the quality of water in rivers, lakes, and groundwater.
  • Set pollution control standards for industries and vehicles.
  • Guide State Pollution Control Boards.
  • Conduct environmental research and laboratory testing.

💖 You May Enjoy This Path If You

  • Care about protecting the environment.
  • Are interested in pollution control and climate issues.
  • Like science, research, and field work.
  • Want to contribute to public health and environmental safety.

📍 Real Life Examples

  • A CPCB officer checks air quality levels and informs the public when pollution increases.
  • An environmental engineer visits factories to make sure pollution control rules are followed.
  • A laboratory staff member tests water samples to check if they are safe.
  • An officer works on plans to reduce waste and keep cities clean.

🎯 How to get a job in CPCB

🎓 Jobs After 12th

Lower Division Clerk (LDC) / Data Entry Operator

  • Eligibility: 12th pass from a recognised board. Basic typing and computer knowledge required.
  • Minimum Marks: No fixed percentage specified; selection is competitive.
  • Age Limit: Usually 18 – 27 years (relaxation as per government rules).
  • Selection: Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by Typing / Skill Test.

Junior Laboratory Assistant

  • Eligibility: 12th pass with Science subjects from a recognised board.
  • Minimum Marks: As mentioned in official recruitment notification.
  • Age Limit: Usually 18 – 27 years (relaxation applicable).
  • Selection: Computer Based Test (CBT). Skill test may be required.

Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) / Field Attendant

  • Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass (as per notification).
  • Minimum Marks: Not specifically fixed.
  • Age Limit: Usually 18 – 27 years (relaxation as per rules).
  • Selection: Computer Based Test (CBT).

🛠️ Roles & Responsibilities (After 12th)

Lower Division Clerk (LDC) / Data Entry Operator

  • Maintain office files, records, and official documents.
  • Enter data collected into computer.
  • Prepare letters, reports, and administrative documents.
  • Assist in daily clerical and office work.

Junior Laboratory Assistant

  • Assist scientists in laboratory testing and experiments.
  • Help in collecting air or water samples.
  • Maintain lab equipment and materials.
  • Recording test results and maintaining lab reports.

Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) / Field Attendant

  • Support field teams during environmental inspections.
  • Help in sample collection and basic field activities.
  • Maintain office or laboratory cleanliness and equipment.
  • Assist in general support tasks assigned by officers.

📘 Exam Details

Lower Division Clerk (LDC) / Data Entry Operator

  • CBT – Computer Based Test: Online exam with multiple choice questions(MCQ) covering General Intelligence, English Language, General Awareness, and Quantitative Aptitude.
  • Typing / Skill Test: Qualifying test to assess typing speed or data entry skills.

Junior Laboratory Assistant

  • CBT – Computer Based Test: Online exam with multiple choice questions(MCQ)covering Science subjects, General Knowledge, and Reasoning ability.
  • Skill Test (if applicable): Practical test to assess laboratory handling skills.

Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) / Field Attendant

  • CBT – Computer Based Test: Online exam with multiple choice questions(MCQ) exam covering General Intelligence, Numerical Ability, and General Awareness.

📌 Selection Process

Lower Division Clerk (LDC) / Data Entry Operator

Step 1 Apply online through the official CPCB recruitment portal when notification is released.
Step 2 Appear for the Computer Based Test (CBT – MCQ format).
Step 3 Qualified candidates attend the Typing / Skill Test.
Step 4 Final merit list prepared based on exam performance.

Junior Technician (Laboratory)

Step 1 Apply online through the official CPCB website.
Step 2 Appear for the Computer Based Test (CBT – MCQ format).
Step 3 Attend Skill / Practical Test (if applicable).
Step 4 Final selection based on overall performance.

Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) / Field Attendant

Step 1 Apply online through the official CPCB recruitment portal.
Step 2 Appear for the Computer Based Test (CBT – MCQ format).
Step 3 Final merit list prepared based on CBT marks.
