🧊 3D Artist

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • When you play a video game like PUBG or watch an animated movie
  • You see characters, buildings, vehicles, and entire environments
  • Someone creates them using 3D software
  • That person is called a 3D Artist
  • 👉 In simple words: 3D artists create everything you see, and 3D animators make them move.

🎓 How to Become a 3D Artist

After completing 10th, you can start learning 3D design, modeling, and visualization through diploma or certificate courses and build strong creative skills.

Diploma in 3D Animation & Modeling

Duration: 1 to 2 Years

Eligibility: Class 10 pass

Exam: Mostly merit-based or direct admission

Certificate in 3D Design / Modeling

Duration: 3 to 6 months

Eligibility: Class 10 pass

Exam: Direct admission

Certificate in 3D Architectural Visualization

Duration: 3 to 6 months

Eligibility: Class 10 pass

Exam: Direct admission

Advanced Diploma in 3D Animation & VFX

Duration: 2 to 3 Years

Eligibility: Class 10 pass

Exam: Merit-based or institute aptitude test

Note: Most 3D design and animation courses after 10th do not require national-level entrance exams. Admission is usually based on basic creativity tests or direct admission.

After completing 12th, you can pursue degree or professional courses in 3D art, animation, and design to build advanced skills and enter the industry.

B.Sc. in Animation & VFX

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 from any stream

Exam: Merit-based or entrance exams like CUET

Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Digital Arts

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 pass

Exam: Entrance exams + portfolio review

Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Animation/Game Art

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 from any stream

Exam: UCEED, NID DAT, or similar design exams

B.Voc in 3D Animation

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 pass

Exam: Merit-based or CUET

Professional Course in Game Asset Design

Duration: 1 to 2 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 pass

Exam: Direct admission or creative interview

📝 Exam Details

(Click on the exam name to see full details)

CUET (Common University Entrance Test) UCEED (Design Entrance Exam) NID DAT (National Institute of Design Aptitude Test) Portfolio Review / Creative Test Direct Admission (Merit-based)

After graduation, you can specialize in advanced 3D art, animation, and design fields to improve your skills and get higher-level job roles.

M.Sc. in 3D Animation & VFX

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any field

Exam: Merit-based or entrance exams like CEED

Post Graduate Diploma (PGD) in 3D Animation

Duration: 1 Year

Eligibility: Graduation in any stream

Exam: Direct admission or institute interview

M.Des in Animation / Game Design

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: Graduation (Design/Fine Arts preferred)

Exam: CEED or institute-level tests

Professional Certificate in Character Sculpting (ZBrush)

Duration: 3 to 6 Months

Where do 3D Artists work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in 3D Art.

You usually start with basic roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

🧊

Junior 3D Modeler

(After Diploma / B.Voc)

Works in gaming studios or advertising companies.

🎨

3D Texture Artist

(After B.Sc / Certificate)

Works in visualization or film production.

💡

3D Lighting Artist

After Diploma in VFX

Works in animation or architectural firms.

📷

3D Scanning Technician

After Certificate / B.Sc

Works in e-commerce or heritage projects.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

👨‍🎨

3D Character Artist

After B.Sc / B.Des + Experience

Works in game or film studios.

🌍

Environment Artist

After B.Des / B.Voc + Experience

Works in gaming studios or virtual productions.

⚪

Look Development Artist

After B.Sc / M.Sc

Works in VFX studios.

🚗

Hard Surface Modeler

After Diploma + Specialization

Works in industries or sci-fi design studios.

🟣 Advanced Roles

📋

Asset Lead

After Graduation + Experience

Manages asset creation team and ensures quality standards.

💻

Technical Artist

After B.Tech / B.Sc

Works between coding and art.

🎬

Art Director

After BFA / B.Des

Decides overall visual style and direction of projects.

🎬
