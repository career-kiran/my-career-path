Merit-Based PG Nursing Admission

Merit-Based PG Nursing Admission means students are selected based on their B.Sc Nursing or Post Basic B.Sc Nursing marks, without writing an entrance examination.

Example:

If 10 students apply for an M.Sc Nursing course and the college has only 5 seats

The college may prepare a merit list based on the B.Sc Nursing marks of these 10 students

The top 5 students with the highest marks in B.Sc Nursing will be offered admission
