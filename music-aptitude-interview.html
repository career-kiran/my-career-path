Music Aptitude Interview

Music Aptitude Interview is a selection process conducted by some colleges, universities, and music institutes for admission to music courses.

During the interview, they check your interest in music, basic musical knowledge, communication skills, confidence, and willingness to learn.

Some institutes may also ask you to sing a few lines or identify simple musical notes.

In simple words, a music aptitude interview is conducted to find out whether you have the interest and potential needed to join a music course.

Example:

A student applies for a Bachelor of Music.

During the interview, they ask about the student's musical background, favourite style of music, and why the student wants to study music.

They make ask the student to sing a song.

Based on the overall performance, the institute decides whether to offer admission.
