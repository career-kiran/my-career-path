🧊 3D Artist

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

"When you play a video game like PUBG or watch an animated movie

You see characters, buildings, vehicles, and entire environments

Someone creates them using 3D software

That person is called a 3D Artist

👉 Example:
In a game like PUBG:
• 3D Artist creates the player, guns, buildings, and vehicles
• 3D Animator makes the player run, jump, shoot, and move

👉 In simple words: 3D artists create everything you see, and 3D animators make them move."

  • Enjoy drawing characters, objects, or environments.

  • Have interest in animation, visual effects, or game design.

These are some roles you can explore in 3D Art.
You usually start with basic roles and grow with experience.

Beginner Roles

  • Junior 3D Modeler (After Diploma / B.Voc) – works in game studios or advertising
    ► creates basic 3D objects like props, furniture, and simple assets
  • 3D Texture Artist (After B.Sc / Certificate) – works in visualization or film production
    ► applies colors, materials, and textures to make models realistic
  • 3D Lighting Artist (After Diploma in VFX) – works in animation or architectural firms
    ► sets lighting to create mood, depth, and realism in scenes
  • 3D Scanning Technician (After Certificate / B.Sc) – works in e-commerce or heritage projects
    ► converts real-world objects into digital 3D models using scanning tools

Mid-Level Roles

  • 3D Character Artist (After B.Sc / B.Des + Experience) – works in game or film studios
    ► creates detailed characters including face, body, clothes, and textures
  • Environment Artist (After B.Des / B.Voc + Experience) – works in gaming or virtual production
    ► designs large 3D environments like cities, forests, and landscapes
  • Look Development Artist (After B.Sc / M.Sc + Specialization) – works in VFX studios
    ► defines final look using textures, shaders, and lighting
  • Hard Surface Modeler (After Diploma + Specialization) – works in industrial or sci-fi design
    ► creates complex models like vehicles, weapons, and machines

Advanced Roles

  • Asset Lead / Supervisor (After Graduation + Experience) – works in leadership roles
    ► manages asset creation team and ensures quality standards
  • Technical Artist (After B.Sc / B.Tech + Experience) – works between art and programming
    ► builds tools and optimizes 3D assets for games and real-time engines
  • Art Director (After BFA / B.Des + Experience) – works in creative leadership roles
    ► decides the visual style and artistic direction of projects
  • International Animation Studios – create 3D characters and environments for animated movies and series.
  • Global Game Development Companies – design 3D characters, objects, and worlds used in video games played around the world.
  • Film and VFX Studios – create realistic 3D models and visual effects for movies and web series.
  • Advertising and Media Companies – design 3D visuals used in advertisements and digital media.
  • Freelance and Remote Work – many 3D artists work with international clients through online platforms.

3D artists create digital characters, objects, and environments used in movies, animation, video games, and advertisements.

Many artists start by learning 3D software and practicing small projects. With experience, they may work in animation studios, game development companies, film production houses, or media agencies.

In this career, creativity, technical skills, and a strong collection of projects are very important. Building a good portfolio of 3D models and animations can help artists get better opportunities.
