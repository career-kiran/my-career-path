Data Analyst

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine a shopping app notices that fewer people are buying products.
  • The company wants to understand why sales are going down.
  • Some experts study numbers, customer information, and sales reports to find the reason.
  • These professionals are called Data Analysts.
  • 👉 In simple words: Data Analysts study data and numbers to help companies make better decisions.

Where do Data Analysts work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in Data Analytics and business intelligence.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

📊

Junior Data Analyst

(After B.Sc. / BCA / Certifications)

Cleans data and tracks business trends.

📑

Research Assistant

(After B.A. / B.Sc. in Economics or Statistics)

Collects data and prepares reports.

🖥️

Operations Analytics Trainee

(After 12th + Analytics Certificate)

Updates spreadsheets and tracks performance.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

📈

Senior Data Analyst

(Graduate Degree + Experience)

Builds dashboards and analyzes databases.

🛒

Market Research Analyst

(MBA / Master’s in Economics)

Studies customer and market trends.

💰

Financial Data Analyst

(B.Com / Finance Degree)

Analyzes budgets and investment data.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🏢

Data Analytics Manager

(5+ Years Experience)

Leads analytics teams and business reporting.

📊

BI Developer

(Advanced Certifications + Experience)

Designs company reporting systems.

🌍

Research Consultant

(Ph.D. / Advanced Master’s)

Advises organizations using research data.
