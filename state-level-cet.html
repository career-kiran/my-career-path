State-Level CET Entrance Exams

State-Level CET Entrance Exams are Common Entrance Tests conducted by various state authorities for admission to different professional courses in India.

Courses includes Engineering, Pharmacy, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Agriculture, Architecture and other professional programmes.

Conducted by: Various state authorities.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Varies by Course and State

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by state)

Duration: Varies by State

Questions: Varies by State

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

State-Level CET Entrance Exams may be used for admission to various professional courses. The courses available may vary from state to state.

Courses may include:
  • Engineering (B.E. / B.Tech)
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Nursing (B.Sc Nursing)
  • Physiotherapy (BPT)
  • Agriculture
  • Architecture
  • Veterinary Science
  • Allied Health Sciences
