DME Admission

DME (Diploma in Marine Engineering) Admission is the admission process for students who wish to join the Diploma in Marine Engineering (DME) course offered by approved maritime training institutes.

The admission process is conducted to select eligible candidates for training as marine engineers in the Merchant Navy.

The admission process may include:

  • Merit-Based Selection
  • Entrance Examination (if applicable)
  • Personal Interview
  • Medical Examination
  • Eyesight and Colour Vision Test
  • Physical Fitness Assessment
  • Document Verification

A student completes the admission process and receives the final selection result.

Based on the student's eligibility, medical fitness, and overall performance, eligible candidates may receive admission to the Diploma in Marine Engineering (DME) course.

Conducted by: Approved maritime training institutes or the respective admission authority.
