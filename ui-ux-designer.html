📱 UI/ UX Designer

🧑‍💻 What Do They Do?

  • You open a food delivery app to order food.
  • You quickly find restaurants, add food to the cart, and pay without confusion.
  • The colors look nice, the buttons are easy to tap, and everything feels smooth.
  • Someone carefully designed that experience so people can use the app easily.
  • That person is called a UI/UX Designer.
    • 👉 In simple words: UI/UX Designers make apps, websites, and digital products easy, attractive, and comfortable to use.

🎓 How to Become a UI/UX Designer

After completing 10th, students can start learning design basics, digital creativity, web design, and user interface fundamentals through diploma or certificate courses.

Diploma in Graphic & Web Design

Duration: 1 Year

Eligibility: Class 10 pass

Exam: Institutional entrance test or interview

Certificate in UI/UX Design Fundamentals

Duration: 6 Months

Eligibility: Class 10 pass

Exam: Direct admission

After completing 12th, students can join degree courses in UI/UX design, interaction design, digital product design, and related technology fields.

Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Interaction Design / User Experience Design

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 pass in any stream

Exam: NID-DAT, UCEED, SEED, or institute entrance exams

B.Sc / B.Voc in UI/UX Design

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 pass

Exam: Merit-based or university entrance exam

B.Tech in Computer Science (with Design Specialization)

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM)

Exam: JEE Main or State CET exams

Duration: 1 Year

Exam: Portfolio review or personal interview

Where do UI/UX Designers work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in UI/UX Design.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🔵 Beginner Roles

🎨

Junior UI/UX Designer

After B.Des / Diploma / Google UX Certificate

Creates basic app screens, wireframes, and simple user-friendly layouts.

📱

UX Design Intern

After Certification / Bootcamp

Supports design teams with user testing, research, and small interface updates.

🖌️

Junior UI Artist

After Diploma / BFA / B.Voc

Designs icons, colors, typography, and visual layouts for apps and websites.

🧩

Product Design Assistant

After Foundation Course / B.Des / B.Tech

Assists senior designers with sketches, prototypes, and research tasks.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

💡

Product Designer

After B.Des + Experience

Designs complete digital products while balancing business and user needs.

🛠️

Interaction Designer

After B.Des / M.Des

Creates smooth animations, transitions, and interactive user experiences.

⚙️

UX Engineer

After B.Tech / UI Experience

Combines coding and design knowledge to improve digital product usability.

🔍

UX Researcher

After B.Des / Psychology + Experience

Studies user behavior and helps teams understand customer problems.

🟣 Advanced Roles

👨‍💼

Lead UX Architect

After M.Des / Senior Experience

Plans the overall structure and navigation flow of large digital platforms.

🧠

Principal Product Designer

After M.Des + Experience

Solves complex design challenges and guides product strategy decisions.

🚀

Head of Design

After Leadership Experience

Leads entire design teams and ensures high-quality creative direction.

🎬

Creative Director

After Senior Industry Experience

Oversees brand visuals, product creativity, and overall user experience vision.

📱

Multinational Tech Firms

Work for companies like Google, Amazon, Microsoft, or Adobe.

🏠

Remote Design Agencies

Collaborate with international design agencies like IDEO or Frog Design.

🛒

E-commerce and Startup Companies

Improve shopping apps, delivery platforms, booking systems for digital businesses.

💳

FinTech and Banking

Design secure and easy-to-use payment apps and money transfer systems.

🎮

Gaming and Entertainment Industry

Create game menus, interactive experiences, streaming platforms.

✈️

International Freelancing

Work independently for global clients through freelance platforms.
