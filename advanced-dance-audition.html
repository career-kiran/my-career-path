Advanced Dance Audition

Advanced Dance Audition is a selection process conducted by some colleges, universities, and dance institutes for admission to advanced dance and choreography programmes.

The audition may include technical dance exercises, learning and performing choreography, improvisation activities, and stage performance. Judges also observe your confidence, discipline, adaptability, and ability to follow instructions.

In simple words, an advanced dance audition is conducted to determine whether a student has the skills and potential required for higher-level dance training.

Example:

Example:

A student applies for a Post Graduate Diploma in Choreography.

The institute asks the student to perform a dance and learn a few new dance steps.

The judges watch how well the student dances, learns new steps, and performs on stage.

Based on the student's performance, the institute decides whether to offer admission.
