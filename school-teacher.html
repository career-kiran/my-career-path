School Teacher

Who is a School teacher?

A school teacher is a person who teaches students in schools and helps them understand subjects clerly and confidently.

What does a School Teacher do?

  • Explains subjects in a simple and step-by-step manner
  • Prepares lesson plans and learning materials
  • Evaluates students through assignments and exams
  • Guides students in both academics and personal growth

You may enjoy this career if you:

  • Enjoy explaining things in a simple ways
  • Like working with children and teenagers
  • Feels happy when others understand because of you

Real Life Examples

  • A school teacher helps students understand a subject(for example,mathematics) step by step
  • a teacher notices a student struggling in class and gives extra support after school to help them improve

Courses to become a Teacher

BA / BSc + B.Ed

Duration: 4-5 years

Eligibility: 12th pass

Minimum marks: 50% aggregate

Admission: Merit-based or University-level entrance exam

B.Ed

Duration: 2 years

Eligibility: Bachelor’s degree in any subject

Admission: University-level entrance exams or merit-based

Integrated BA-B.Ed / BSc-B.Ed

Duration: 4 years

Eligibility: 12th pass

Minimum marks: 50% aggregate

Admission: CUET or University-level entrance exams or merit-based

Where do they work?

  • Primary School Teacher - in Government and private primary schools
  • High School Teacher - in secondary and higher secondary schools
  • Subject Teacher - in CBSE, ICSE and State Board schools
  • Academic Coordinator - supervising teaching methods and curriculum planning
  • Online Tutor - teaching students through online platforms

🌍 Global Opportunities

  • International School Teacher – schools following Indian or International curriculum abroad
  • Education Program Instructor – NGOs and global education organizations
Important Note: Teaching is a career built on patience,continuous learning and passion for helping others. Higher qualifications and teaching experience improve career growth and salary opportunities.
