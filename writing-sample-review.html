Writing Sample Review

Writing Sample Review is an admission process conducted by some colleges, universities, and training institutes before giving admission to courses related to writing, journalism, literature, content creation, and other communication fields.

The purpose of the review is to assess a student's writing skills, creativity, grammar, vocabulary, and ability to express ideas clearly.

Students may be asked to write a short story, essay, article, blog, or opinion on a given topic.

Example:

A student applies for a Creative Writing Certificate Programme.

The institute asks the student to write a short story or an article on a given topic.

The institute evaluates the student's writing style, creativity, grammar, and communication skills.

Based on the student's performance, the institute decides whether to offer admission or not.
