Singer

🎤 What Do They Do?

  • You have listened to songs in movies, albums, or music streaming platforms.
  • Someone is singing behind the scenes to create those musical performances.
  • These professionals are called Singers or Vocal Artists.
  • 👉 In simple words: Singers perform songs using their voice for films, concerts, music albums, live shows, social media, and digital music platforms.

Where do Singers work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in singing, music performance, recording, and vocal entertainment.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

🎶

Chorus Singer / Backing Vocalist

(After Certificate in Music / Singing Popular Music)

They sing support melodies and harmonies behind a lead artist.

🎤

Jingle / Scratch Vocalist

(After Diploma in Light Music / Vocal Training Masterclass)

They record short vocal tracks for commercials or rough samples for music directors.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

🎸

Live Band Vocalist

(After BA (Hons) in Music / Singing Techniques)

They perform songs live at corporate events, festivals, and music venues.

🌐

Independent Indie Artist

(After BPA in Vocal Music / Singing and Vocal Performance)

They write, sing, and release their own original music on streaming platforms.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🎬

Playback Singer

(After MPA in Vocal / Indian Classical Music Foundations)

They record songs for movies, albums, and professional studio productions.

🎼

Vocal Coach / Music Educator

(After MA in Music)

They train singers in vocal techniques, breathing control, and music theory.

🎬

International Music & Film Industries

They hire singers for movie soundtracks, albums, concerts, and studio recordings.

🎧

Music Streaming Platforms

Singers release songs globally through platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube Music.

🌍

International Concerts & Live Shows

Singers perform at global music festivals, tours, cultural programs, and live entertainment events.

🎤

Independent Music & Online Content

Many singers build international audiences through social media, music apps, and original online content.
