Advocate

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • You might have seen advocates in movies or in real life.
  • Advocates help solve people's problems legally.
  • They study law and represent people in court.
  • They also give legal advice and help people understand their rights.
  • 👉 In simple words: Advocates help people solve their problems in court.

🎓 How to Become a Advocate
