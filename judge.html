⚖️ Judge

👤 Who is a Judge?

A judge is a person who listens to legal cases in court and makes fair decisions based on the law.

They make sure justice is given according to the rules of the country.

🛡️ What does a Judge do?

  • Listens to both sides in a court case
  • Studies laws and evidence
  • Gives judgments and sentences
  • Makes sure court procedures are followed properly

💖 You may enjoy this profession if:

  • You believe in fairness and justice
  • You like understanding laws deeply
  • You can stay calm and neutral while making decisions

💡 Real Life Examples

  • A judge listens to a property dispute between two families and decides who legally owns the land.
  • A judge hears a criminal case and decides whether the accused is guilty or not based on evidence.

    📝 Exam Full Forms

    Judicial Services Exam – Conducted by State Public Service Commissions for recruitment of Civil Judges.

    🎓 Minimum Qualification

    • LLB Degree (3-year or 5-year)
    • Enrollment with State Bar Council

    🏢 Where do they work?

    • Civil Judge / Judicial Magistrate – in District Courts
    • District Judge – in District & Sessions Courts
    • High Court Judge – in High Courts
    • Supreme Court Judge – in the Supreme Court of India

    🌍 Global Opportunities

    • Judge / Magistrate – in national courts of other countries (after clearing their judicial qualification process)
    • Legal Advisor – in international courts and organizations

    ⭐ Career Insight

    Becoming a judge requires patience, deep legal knowledge, and strong integrity.
