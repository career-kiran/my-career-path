AIIMS B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam

What is AIIMS B.Sc Nursing Entrance Exam?

AIIMS B.Sc Nursing Entrance Examination is a national-level entrance examination conducted for admission to B.Sc Nursing courses offered by various AIIMS institutes across India.

Conducted by: All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: 12th Pass with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 2 Hours

Questions: 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Frequency: Once a year (usually in June)

Qualification: Passed or appearing in 12th with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English.

Age: Minimum 17 years as on or before 31st December of the admission year.

Minimum Marks:

  • 55% - General / OBC / EWS Category
  • 50% - SC / ST Category

Gender: B.Sc (Hons.) Nursing is generally open to female candidates.

Nationality: Indian Citizen.

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 2 Hours

Total Questions: 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Total Marks: 100 Marks

Correct Answer: +1 Mark

Incorrect Answer: -1/3 Mark

Unanswered Questions: 0 Marks

Questions are asked from Physics, Chemistry, Biology and General Knowledge.

After clearing the AIIMS Nursing Entrance Examination, students can get admission to B.Sc Nursing programmes offered by AIIMS institutes.
