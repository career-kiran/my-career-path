Advanced Audition

Advanced Audition is a selection process conducted by some colleges, universities, and performing arts institutes for admission to advanced performing arts courses.

This audition is mainly conducted to check whether a student has the advanced skills needed to join a higher-level course.

The audition may include:

• Performing a difficult song, dance, music, or acting scene
• Learning and performing a new activity during the audition
• Performing in more than one style
• Showing confidence, creativity, and expression
• Answering a few simple questions about your performance

In simple words, an advanced audition is conducted to find out whether a student is ready to study an advanced performing arts course.

Example:

A student applies for a Post Graduate Diploma in Performing Arts.

During the audition, the student performs a song, dance, music or acting scene.

The judges may also give the student a short activity to perform.

They check the student's skills, confidence, creativity, and overall performance.

Based on the performance, the institute decides whether to offer admission.
