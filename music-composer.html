Music Composer

🧑‍🎼 What Do They Do?

  • You have listened to background music in movies, web series, games, or advertisements.
  • Someone creates the melodies and emotional music behind those scenes.
  • These professionals are called Music Composers.
  • 👉 In simple words: Music Composers create tunes, background scores, and original music using instruments, creativity, and music software.

Where do Music Composers work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in music composition, film scoring, music production, and soundtrack creation.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🟢 Beginner Roles

🎹

Assistant Music Programmer

(After Certificate in Music Production / Introduction to Music Production)

They create basic beats, synth layers, and digital music tracks to support senior composers.

🎬

Background Score Arranger

(After Diploma in Music Production / Electronic Music Production)

They compose short background sound layers and mood tracks for scenes and videos.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

📺

Jingle Composer

(After BA (Hons) in Music Production / Music Composition Masterclass)

They compose catchy melodies and soundtracks for advertisements and commercials.

🌐

Independent Music Producer

(After BPA in Music Composition / Film Scoring and Composition)

They compose, arrange, and release original music on streaming platforms.

🟣 Advanced Roles

🎼

Film Music Director

(After MPA in Music Composition / Composing for Orchestras)

They create soundtracks, theme songs, and background scores for movies and web series.

🎻

Orchestrator / Conductor

(After MA in Music)

They arrange musical compositions for orchestras and guide live musicians during performances.

🎬

International Film Industries

To compose songs, background scores, and soundtracks for movies and series.

🎧

Streaming & Music Platforms

They work with composers and music producers to create original albums and digital music content.

🌐

Gaming & Animation Studios

To create emotional background music and cinematic soundtracks for games and animation.

🎼

Live Concerts & International Projects

They collaborate with orchestras, singers, and global artists for concerts, albums, and music tours.
