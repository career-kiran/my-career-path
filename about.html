About This Website

Choosing a career can feel confusing, stressful, and overwhelming—especially when there is pressure to decide early or follow what others expect.

My Career Path was created to help students understand themselves first, before choosing a course or profession.

This website does not tell you what you must become. Instead, it gently shows you different career directions, roles, courses, and educational pathways, so you can make informed decisions at your own pace.

There is no hurry here. You don't have to decide everything today. Many people take time to discover what they enjoy—and that's completely okay.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students explore careers with clarity, confidence, and curiosity by providing simple, reliable, and easy-to-understand career guidance.

What You'll Find Here

  • Careers explained in simple language
  • Courses after Class 10, Class 12, and graduation
  • Entrance exam details
  • Top colleges and institutions for different courses
  • Self-reflection questions to help you discover whether a career is right for you
  • Official website links for entrance exams and institutions

This website is designed to feel like a calm guide—not a coaching centre, not an exam portal, and not a comparison race.

Take your time. Explore. Learn. Come back whenever you need. Your path will become clearer, one step at a time.
