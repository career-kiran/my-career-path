About this website

Choosing a career can feel confusing, stressful, and overwhelming — especially when there is pressure to decide early or follow what others expect.

My Career Path was created to help students understand themselves first, before choosing a course or profession.

This website does not tell you what you must become. Instead, it gently shows you different directions, roles, and learning paths, so you can make informed decisions at your own pace.

There is no hurry here. You don’t have to decide everything today. Many people take time to discover what they enjoy — and that’s completely okay.

This website is designed to feel like a calm guide — not a coaching centre, not an exam portal, and not a comparison race.

Take your time. Explore. Come back again. Your path will become clearer step by step.
