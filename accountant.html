Accountant

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • Imagine you own a small shop.
  • Every day, customers buy products from your shop.
  • As customers keep buying, the products in your shop become less.
  • So, you buy more products from suppliers or wholesalers to fill your shop again.
  • Money comes in when customers buy products, and money goes out when you buy new products.
  • It is important to keep a correct record of all this money.
  • An Accountant keeps these money records and makes sure everything is correct.
  • 👉 In simple words: Accountants keep track of the money coming in and the money going out, whether it is a small shop or a large company.
