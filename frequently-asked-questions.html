Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are answers to some common questions about My Career Path. If you still have a question, please visit our Contact page.

1. What is My Career Path?

My Career Path is a free career guidance website that helps students explore careers, courses, entrance exams, subjects, and educational pathways in simple language.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes. All the information on this website is available free of cost.

3. Who is this website for?

This website is mainly designed for students studying in Class 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, graduates, parents, teachers, and anyone exploring different career options.

4. How do I choose the right career?

Start by understanding your interests, strengths, and what kind of work you enjoy. This website is designed to help you explore different careers so you can make informed decisions at your own pace.

5. Are the career, college, and exam details accurate?

We try our best to keep the information accurate and up to date. However, admission rules, eligibility criteria, entrance exams, fees, and other details may change over time.

Always verify important information through the official websites of colleges, universities, and examination authorities.

6. Can I suggest a new career or report an error?

Yes. We welcome your suggestions and feedback. If you find any incorrect information or would like us to include a new career or course, please contact us through our Contact page.

7. Are the career recommendations based on a test?

No. The reflection questions on this website are designed to help you think about your interests and understand whether a career may suit you. They are not career prediction or aptitude tests.

8. Do you guarantee admission into colleges or jobs?

No. This website provides educational and career guidance only. Admission into colleges and future employment depend on eligibility, preparation, performance, and the selection process of individual institutions or employers.

9. How often is the website updated?

We regularly review and update the content whenever important information changes or when new careers, courses, or entrance exams are added.

10. Can I share this website with my friends or school?

Absolutely! If you find My Career Path helpful, feel free to share it with your friends, teachers, parents, and schools.

Still Have a Question?
