🌟 Discover Yourself

Complete these 3 simple activities before exploring careers.

  • The purpose of these activities is not to choose a career today.
  • This is to help you discover your true interests, passions, and aspirations.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. Be honest with yourself.
  • I strongly encourage you to spend at least 3 to 10 days reflecting on these questions before making a final career decision.

    • What Makes You Happy?

    STEP 1:

    Take a pen and paper.

    STEP 2:

    Think about those activities that make you really happy while doing them

    STEP 3:

    Write down as many activities as you can think of.

    Examples:

    • Drawing
    • Helping someone
    • Playing a musical instrument
    • Writing stories
    • Coding
    • Teaching others
    • Repairing gadgets
    • Playing sports
    • Designing things

    💡 These are just examples. Your interests may be completely different, and that's perfectly okay.

    ❌ Don't include passive activities like watching TV, scrolling social media, or eating your favorite food.

    What You like to do in Life?

    STEP 1:

    Take a pen and paper.

    STEP 2:

    Ask this question to yourself:

    "If money is not the only goal, then what would I do in life?"

    STEP 3:

    Write down all the ideas that come to your mind.

    What Makes Your Life Meaningful?

    STEP 1:

    Take a pen and paper.

    STEP 2:

    Imagine you are 70 years old.

    STEP 3:

    Ask yourself:

    "Did I spent my life doing something I truly loved?"

    STEP 4:

    So choose a career that excites you, and gives you a sense of purpose.

    STEP 5:

    Write down all the ideas that comes to your mind.
