🌟 Discover Yourself

Complete these simple activities before exploring careers.

  • The purpose of these activities is not to choose a career today.
  • This is to help you discover your true interests, passions, and aspirations.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. Be honest with yourself.
  • I strongly encourage you to spend at least 3 to 10 days reflecting on these questions before making a final career decision.

    • STEP 1:

    Take a pen and paper

    STEP 2:

    Think about all the activities that make you feel happy while doing them

    STEP 3:

    Write down every activity you enjoy

    Example:

    Drawing, Helping someone, Playing a musical instrument, Writing stories, Coding, Teaching others, Repairing gadgets, Playing sports, Designing things

    💡 These are just examples. Your interests may be completely different, and that's perfectly okay.

    ❌ Don't include passive activities like watching TV, scrolling social media, or eating your favorite food.

    STEP 4:

    Read your list again

    For each activity, ask yourself:

    "Why do I enjoy doing this?"

    Example:

    • I like drawing because I enjoy being creative.
    • I like teaching because I enjoy helping others.
    • I like coding because I enjoy solving problems.
    💡 Don't focus only on the activity. Focus on why you enjoy it. This will help you choose a career that matches your interests.

    This activity will help you identify the types of work you don't enjoy.

    This can help you avoid choosing a career you may not like.

    STEP 1:

    Take a pen and paper

    STEP 2:

    Think about the types of work you don't enjoy

    Examples:

    • I don't enjoy taking care of sick people.
    • I don't enjoy repairing machines.
    • I don't enjoy teaching others.
    • I don't enjoy speaking in front of large groups.
    • I don't enjoy doing calculations.
    • I don't enjoy sitting in front of a computer all day.
    • I don't enjoy working outdoors.
    ❌ Don't think about careers. Think about the type of work.

    STEP 3:

    Now write down all the types of work you dont like

    💡 These are just examples. You write down the types of work that comes to your mind.

    STEP 4:

    Read your list again.

    For each type of work, ask yourself:

    "Why don't I enjoy this?"

    Examples:

    • I don't enjoy teaching because I don't like speaking in front of a class.
    • I don't enjoy repairing machines because I don't like fixing things.
    • I don't enjoy looking after sick people because I feel uncomfortable.
    💡 Don't focus only on the type of work. Focus on why you don't enjoy it. This will help you choose a career that matches your interests.
