🩺 NEET(National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

🎯 What is NEET?

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) is a national-level entrance exam for students who want to study medical courses in India.

It is conducted by the National Testing Agency(NTA).

🎓 Courses Through NEET

Through NEET, students can get admission to various medical courses in India.

Courses includes:-
  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery)
  • BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)
  • BAMS (Ayurveda)
  • BHMS (Homeopathy)
  • BUMS (Unani)
  • BVSc (Veterinary Science)

✅ Eligibility

Qualification: Passed or appearing in Plus Two with Physics, Chemistry, Biology.Appearing = currently in Class 12 (writing exams this year)

Age: Minimum 17 years (There is no upper age limit).

Minimum Marks:

  • 50% - General category
  • 40% - SC/ST/OBC
  • 45% - PwD (Persons with Disability)

Attempts: No limit on the number of attempts.

🧾 Exam Pattern

Mode: Offline (Pen & Paper)

Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Exam Frequency: Conducted once a year

Duration: 3 hours (PwD: 4 hours with extra time)

Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology

Total Questions: 180 (All compulsory)

Total Marks: 720 Marks

Marking Scheme:

  • +4 - for every correct answer
  • -1 - for every wrong answer
  • 0 - for unattempted questions

📊 Subject-wise Distribution

  • Physics: 45 Questions (180 Marks)
  • Chemistry: 45 Questions (180 Marks)
  • Biology: 90 Questions (360 Marks)
    (Botany: 45 + Zoology: 45)

Total: 180 Questions | 720 Marks

📝 Application Process

Mode: Online application

Official Websites: neet.nta.nic.in 🔗 | nta.ac.in 🔗

Steps to Apply:

  • Register: Create an account using your name, mobile number, and email ID.
  • Fill Form: Enter personal details, academic details, and choose exam city.
  • Upload Documents: Upload photo, signature, and required certificates.
  • Pay Fee: Pay the application fee online (UPI / Card / Net Banking).
  • Confirmation: Submit the form and download the confirmation page.

Documents Required:

  • Passport size photograph
  • Signature
  • Class 10 & 12 details
  • Valid ID proof

(Note: Fill the form carefully. Incorrect details may lead to rejection.)

For more details and latest updates, visit the official NEET website or NTA website.
