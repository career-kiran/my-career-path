University Entrance Exams

University-Level Entrance Exams are entrance examinations conducted by universities for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate courses.

courses includes Engineering, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Pharmacy, Allied Health Sciences, Management and other professional programmes.

Conducted by: Various universities and educational institutions.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: Varies by Course and University

Mode: Online or Offline (varies by university)

Duration: Varies by University

Questions: Varies by University

Frequency: Usually conducted once a year

University-Level Entrance Exams may be used for admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate courses. The courses available may vary from one university to another.

Courses may include:
  • Engineering (B.Tech & M.Tech)
  • Nursing (B.Sc Nursing & M.Sc Nursing)
  • Physiotherapy (BPT & MPT)
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm & M.Pharm)
  • Allied Health Sciences
  • Management (BBA & MBA)
  • Agriculture
  • Architecture (B.Arch & M.Arch)
  • Science Courses (B.Sc & M.Sc)
  • Arts and Humanities Courses (BA & MA)
