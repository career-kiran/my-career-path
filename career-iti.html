🛠️ ITI

ITI is a short-term, hands-on course where you learn a skill and get ready to start working quickly.

It focuses more on practical work, and most courses last between 6 months to 2 years.

If you join an ITI course, you can do the following jobs.

⚡ Electrician

Install and repair electrical wiring and equipment.

🔩 Fitter

Work with machines and assemble metal parts.

🔥 Welder

Join metal parts using welding techniques.

🔧 Mechanic

Repair and maintain vehicles like bikes and cars.

🚰 Plumber

Install and fix water pipes and drainage systems.

🪚 Carpenter

Make and repair furniture and wooden structures.

❄️ AC Technician

Install and repair air conditioners and refrigerators.

⚙️ Machinist

Operate machines to shape metal parts.
