Actor

🧑‍💻 What Do They Do?

  • When you watch a movie, web series, or theatre performance, someone is bringing the characters and emotions to life on screen or stage.
  • These professionals are called Actors.
  • 👉 In simple words: Actors perform stories, dialogues, and emotions for movies, television, theatre, advertisements, and digital entertainment.

Where do Actors work? (Job Roles + Growth)

These are some roles you can explore in acting and performance.

You usually start with beginner roles and grow with experience.

🔵 Beginner Roles

🎭

Background Actor / Extra

(After Short-Term / Online Course)

Performs crowd scenes and supports background action.

📺

Junior Actor

(After Certificate / Diploma)

Performs small roles in films and television.

🔵 Mid-Level Roles

🎬

Supporting Actor

(After Degree / Diploma + Experience)

Plays important secondary characters in stories.

🎙️

Voice-Over / Dubbing Artist

(After Certificate / Diploma + Experience)

Gives voice performances for films and animation.

🟣 Advanced Roles

⭐

Lead Actor

(After PG Diploma / Advanced Degree + Experience)

Plays major characters and leads the storyline.

🎥

Theatre Director

(After Post Graduate Program + Experience)

Guides performances and manages stage productions.

🎬

Global Film Studios

They hire actors for films, series, and global productions.

📺

OTT & Streaming Platforms

They cast actors for Netflix, Amazon Prime, and Disney+ shows.

🎙️

Voice Acting Platforms

They offer dubbing and voice-over work for global media.

🌐

International Talent Agencies

They connect actors with worldwide auditions and projects.
