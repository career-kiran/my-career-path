Portfolio Review

What is a Portfolio? A portfolio is a collection of a student's best creative work. It may include drawings, paintings, photographs, animations, designs, models, videos, or other projects created by the student.

Portfolio Review is an admission process where colleges look at a student's creative work before offering admission.

Students usually do not need to write an entrance exam.

Example:

A student wants to join a Design course.

Instead of writing an entrance exam, the college asks the student to submit a portfolio.

Teachers and experts review the portfolio to understand the student's creativity, skills, and potential.

If the portfolio is impressive, the student may receive admission or be invited for an interview.
