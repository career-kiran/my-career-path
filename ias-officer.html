IAS Officer

🧑‍🎨 What Do They Do?

  • IAS stands for Indian Administrative Service.
  • The word "Administration" means managing and supervising work.
  • The government has many departments like education, health, police, transport, and agriculture.
  • An IAS Officer manages these government departments and makes sure they work properly.
  • They also make sure government projects and services reach the people.
  • 👉 In simple words: IAS Officers manage government departments and make sure government work is carried out properly.

🎓 How to Become an IAS Officer

STEP 1

Complete your Class 12 from a recognized board. You can choose Arts, Science, or Commerce.

STEP 2

Complete a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. (B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., B.Tech., M.B.B.S or any other bachelor's degree)

STEP 3

Apply for the UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE).

UPSC Civil Services Examination
STEP 4

Prepare for the Preliminary Examination (Prelims) and pass the exam.

STEP 5

Prepare for the Main Examination (Mains) and pass the exam.

STEP 6

Attend the Personality Test (Interview) conducted by UPSC.

STEP 7

If selected, complete the IAS training provided by the Government of India and become an IAS Officer.
