INI-CET Dental (Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test)

What is INI-CET?

INI-CET Dental is a national-level postgraduate dental entrance examination for students who have completed the BDS course and wish to pursue postgraduate dental courses in India's top medical institutions.

Conducted by: AIIMS, New Delhi for admission to postgraduate courses in premier dental institutes like:

  • AIIMS New Delhi, PGIMER Chandigarh, JIPMER Puducherry and other participating AIIMS institutes.

📌 Quick Facts

Eligibility: BDS Degree

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 3 Hours

Total Questions: 200

Frequency: Twice a year (January and July)

Qualification: BDS Degree from a recognised medical college.

Internship: Must have completed the compulsory internship as prescribed by AIIMS.

Registration: Must possess provisional or permanent medical registration.

Age: No upper age limit.

Attempts: No limit on the number of attempts.

Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Duration: 3 Hours

Total Questions: 200 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Total Marks: 200 Marks

Correct Answer: +1 Mark

Incorrect Answer: -1/3 Mark

Unanswered Questions: 0 Marks

Questions are asked from various subjects covered during the BDS course, including both pre-clinical and clinical subjects.

After clearing INI-CET Dental, students can get admission to various postgraduate dental courses in Top institutes of India.

Courses include:-
  • MDS (Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics)
  • MDS (Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics)
  • MDS (Prosthodontics and Crown & Bridge)
  • MDS (Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery)
  • MDS (Periodontology)
  • MDS (Paediatric and Preventive Dentistry)
  • MDS (Oral Medicine and Radiology)
  • MDS (Oral Pathology and Microbiology)
  • MDS (Public Health Dentistry)
