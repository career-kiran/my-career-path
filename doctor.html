Doctor

🩺 What Do They Do?

  • If you have a high fever or any health problem, you will usually go to a hospital or clinic
  • There....someone examines you, checks your symptoms, and finds out what problem you have
  • They may give medicines, treatments, or advice to help you recover
  • That person is called a Doctor
  • 👉 In simple words: Doctors find out health problems and treat people to help them stay healthy

🎓 How to Become a Doctor

These medical degree programs help students learn about the human body, diseases, diagnosis, treatments, surgeries, patient care, and healthcare systems.

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery)

Duration: 5.5 Years (Including 1 Year Internship)

Eligibility: 12th Pass with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (Minimum 50% Marks)

Exam: NEET-UG

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: MBBS Graduate (With Completed Internship)
