Doctor

A doctor diagnoses illnesses, treats patients, and helps people recover and stay healthy. Doctors work closely with patients and play an important role in saving lives.

You may enjoy this career if you…

  • Want to help people when they are sick
  • Are ready to study for many years
  • Can stay calm in stressful situations
  • Feel responsible for others’ well-being

Courses to become a Doctor (India)

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine & Bachelor of Surgery)

Duration: 5.5 years (4.5 years study + 1 year internship)

Eligibility:

  • 12th standard with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum marks: 50% (General category)

Entrance exam: NEET-UG

NEET-UG NEET-UG is a national-level entrance exam required for admission to MBBS in government and private medical colleges in India.

Where do Doctors work?

  • Hospitals and clinics
  • Government health services
  • Private practice
  • Medical colleges and research institutions

Important note

Becoming a doctor requires long-term commitment, dedication, and continuous learning. It is a demanding career that involves responsibility for patient's health and well-being.
